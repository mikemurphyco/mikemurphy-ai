### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week:**UGC

 UGC stands for **User-Generated Content** — and it’s the lifeblood of modern online platforms.
Content created by users through AI tools and platforms, including text, images, videos, or other media. In the context of AI, this often refers to prompts, AI-generated artwork, or other creative outputs that users produce using AI tools like DALL-E, Midjourney, or ChatGPT. This term highlights the shift from passive consumption to active creation using AI technologies.

 AI tools now help creators generate visuals, voices, and full-on characters in minutes — meaning the next viral clip might be made by you… or your AI alter ego.
