- **Availability:** ChatKit and new Evals are GA; Agent Builder is beta; Connector Registry rolling out in beta

### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week: Semantic Search**

**Semantic search** is a search technique that aims to understand the meaning and intent behind a query, rather than just matching keywords. It uses natural language processing and machine learning to interpret context, relationships between words, and user intent to deliver more relevant results.

 Think of semantic search like a librarian who understands what you're really asking for, rather than just matching the exact words you say.

**Semantic Search **is why modern AI assistants can understand what you're asking for and pull relevant information, even when you phrase things in unexpected ways.

 For example, if you ask "How do I reset my password?", semantic search might also find helpful articles titled "Trouble logging in?" or "Account access recovery" because it understands these topics are related in meaning, not just in the words used.

### 🧰 AI Tool of The Week: ****OpenRouter

[OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-27-openai-strikes-again) is a unified API that lets you access and route across many LLMs.

 OpenRouter provides a single, OpenAI‑compatible interface to hundreds of AI models from major providers. It focuses on reliability, price/performance, and governance so you can swap models, fail over when a provider is down, and enforce data policies without changing your app’s core integration.
