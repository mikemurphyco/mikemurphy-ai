### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week:**Prompt Engineering

**Prompt:**
A prompt is the text input or instruction you give to an AI system to tell it what you want it to do. For example, “Write a summary of this article.”

**Prompt Engineering:**
Prompt engineering is the practice of crafting effective prompts to get better results from AI models and tools. This includes how to structure your requests, provide the right context, and use specific techniques to guide the AI toward producing more accurate, useful, or creative outputs. Good prompt engineering can mean the difference between getting a generic response and getting exactly what you need.
