### 🗞️******AI News of The Week:****The OpenAI 'Code Red' Memo Story**

 Is Sam Altman getting nervous about its AI Competitors?

**OpenAI Declares 'Code Red' Amid AI Race Intensification**

 OpenAI CEO Sam Altman issued an internal "Code Red" memo (the company's highest urgency level) in response to mounting competitive pressure from Google's Gemini 3 and rivals like Anthropic, which are now outperforming OpenAI in key benchmarks.

- **Competitive Pressure:** The move is a direct reaction to Google's **Gemini 3** and the growing traction of rivals like [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-35-code-red), which have begun to outperform OpenAI's models in key industry benchmarks.

 The company is entering triage mode, halting nearly all non-core initiatives (AI agents, advertising, personal assistants) to focus exclusively on improving ChatGPT's speed, reliability, and user experience. This strategic retreat comes after significant talent loss to competing labs and represents OpenAI's effort to defend its market position in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

🧠******AI Term of The Week:****Mixture of Experts (MoE)**

 Mixture of Experts (MoE) is a clever way to build giant models without running the whole thing every time. An MoE model contains many small “expert” sub-networks and a gating system that picks just 2-8 of them per query.

- Trillions of parameters, but only billions active → fast + cheap

- Powers Mistral, Grok-3, DeepSeek-V3, and rumored GPT-5

- Why your phone can now run 100B+ class models

 Think specialist chefs in a kitchen instead of one overworked generalist.
