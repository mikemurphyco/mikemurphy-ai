### 🗞️ AI News of The Week: Apple Picks Google Gemini for Siri

**Apple Partners with Google for Gemini-Powered Siri** Apple has officially announced a multiyear partnership with Google to use Gemini models as the foundation for a major AI-powered [Siri](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/30/apple-intelligence-ai-siri-iphone.html?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-41-will-siri-finally-be-good) upgrade launching later this year. This collaboration will leverage Google's cloud and model capabilities while Apple continues to utilize its own on-device processing and private cloud compute.

- **Gemini Foundation:**[Google’s Gemini](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/08/google-adds-gemini-features-to-gmail-message-summaries-proofreading-.html?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-41-will-siri-finally-be-good) models will power the new iteration of Siri, unlocking innovative experiences for users.

- **Hybrid Infrastructure:** The deal integrates Google's cloud strengths with Apple's existing privacy-focused on-device models.

- **Open AI Strategy:** Apple confirmed this new deal does not change its existing partnership with [OpenAI](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/16/openai-in-talks-with-amazon-about-investment-could-top-10-billion.html?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-41-will-siri-finally-be-good) regarding ChatGPT integration.

**Mike’s Take:**

- **Surprising:**Apple seems to be prioritizing user experience of ego by outsourcing AI to Google and not trying to build their own solution in-house. Almost seems like Apple realizes how far behind in the AI race they really are.

- **Big Win for Google:** Gemini gets onto billions of iPhones validates Google's model quality and keeps them neck-and-neck with OpenAI in the race for consumer AI dominance.

### 🧠 AI Term of The Week: Context Window

**Context window** is the maximum amount of information an AI model can "see" and process at one time. When you chat with Claude, everything in that conversation (your messages, Claude's responses, uploaded files, tool outputs) takes up space in this window

 👉 Once you exceed the context window, the AI starts "forgetting" earlier parts of the conversation as new information pushes old information out.

**For Vibe Coders:**
You can now show Claude your entire project structure, multiple configuration files, error logs, AND have a natural conversation about what you're trying to build - all simultaneously. The AI "sees" everything at once.

**Pro tip:** The bigger the context window, the more you can "show not tell." Stop writing lengthy explanations - just dump your files, configs, and logs into the chat. Let the AI's context window do the heavy lifting.

**Bottom line:** Context windows transformed AI from "helpful assistant with amnesia" to "collaborative partner who actually remembers the whole project." That's why vibe coding works in 2025 but would've been impossible in 2022.
