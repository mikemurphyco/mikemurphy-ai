### 🧠 AI Term of The Wee: AI Agents

**AI Agents** is a buzzword that confuses many (including me), but is taking the AI world by storm.

**AI Agent:** is an autonomous system that can **perceive, plan, and act** in a given environment to accomplish a goal or task on your behalf.

 Think of an AI Agents as a mini digital assistant that doesn’t just answer questions, but actually takes steps for you — whether that’s booking a flight, editing a video, or running a full product launch.

 It’s the future of automation… and the future is already knocking.

**Real-world takeaway:** AI Agents are becoming accessible to solopreneurs and creators. Tools like [AutoGPT](https://agpt.co/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=ai-unplugged-issue-no-03) and [CrewAI](https://www.crewai.com/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=ai-unplugged-issue-no-03) (multi-agent collaboration!) are starting to feel less like science fiction and more like Swiss army knives for your creative business.
