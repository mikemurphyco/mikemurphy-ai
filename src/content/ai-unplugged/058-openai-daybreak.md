### 🧠 AI Term of The Week: **AI SEO**

**Definition:**
AI SEO is the practice of optimizing your content so it gets surfaced, cited, and recommended by AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews, not just traditional search results.

**Real examples:**

- Writing a clear, structured comparison guide that ChatGPT can quote when a user asks "what's the best coffee maker for a small office".

- Publishing FAQ-style answers and authoritative content that AI engines treat as a trusted source.

**Why it matters now:**

- Search behavior is shifting. People are asking ChatGPT and Claude instead of typing into Google.

- Being cited by an AI is the new front page. If the AI doesn't mention you, you're invisible.

- Small businesses and solopreneurs can compete.

- Authority and clarity beat domain age in AI answers.
