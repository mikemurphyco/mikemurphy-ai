🧠******AI Term of The Week:****ReRanking (in RAG)**

**ReRanking** is the secret weapon in RAG Pipelines that helps AI choose the best answers, not just the first ones it finds.

 When AI retrieves multiple documents or snippets, a reranker scores and sorts them by how relevant they are to your question or prompt. This means more accurate, helpful, and context-aware responses.

**Why it matters for RAG creators:**

- Better quality content from your custom AI tools

- Less “hallucination” and fluff in AI outputs

- Smarter chatbots, research assistants, and knowledge workflows

- Can boost performance of personal knowledge bases and GPTs

### What is an IDE?

 An **IDE**, or **I**ntegrated **D**evelopment **E**nvironment, is a software application for developers to build apps.

**Key features:**

- **Source code editor:** For writing and editing code.

- **Build automation tools:** For compiling, interpreting, or transforming source code into executable computer programs.

- **Debugger:** For testing and finding errors in the code.

 In essence, an IDE provides the **framework where developers write, test, and package their applications.**
