**Mike’s Take:**
This feels like a big deal. The Genesis Mission is the US Government saying:
“We’re all in and we’re reorganizing the science machine around AI.”

**What is it?**
The White House launched the *[Genesis Mission](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/11/launching-the-genesis-mission/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-34-happy-thanksgiving)*, a sweeping national initiative to supercharge scientific discovery with AI. Think “Manhattan Project energy,” but aimed at breakthroughs in fields like biotechnology, clean energy, quantum science, advanced manufacturing, and more.The plan pulls together federal datasets, national labs, supercomputers, and private-sector partners into one unified AI research platform run by the Department of Energy.
**The goal:**
Accelerate major scientific advances, tighten security around critical tech, and keep the U.S. competitive in a world racing to build AI-powered innovation.

 It’s the biggest U.S. push yet to use AI as a national engine for scientific progress.

🧠******AI Term of The Week:****Function Calling (aka Tool Calling)**

 Function Calling is what lets AI go from just chatting… to actually *doing* things.

 With function calling, you can let AI trigger real code functions like sending an email, adding a calendar event, or fetching live data from an API…all from natural language.

**For example:**
“Book me a meeting with Alex for Friday.”
With Function Calling, the AI doesn't just reply…it runs a function to create that event.

**Why it matters for AI builders:**
Function Calling is the bridge between language and action. It’s how you turn chatbots into full-blown AI agents and build apps that *do* things, not just talk.
