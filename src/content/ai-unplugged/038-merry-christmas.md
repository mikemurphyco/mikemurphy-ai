 🗞️******AI News of The Week:****Your Year with ChatGPT**

 Like nearly every tech company out there, OpenAI rolled out a year in review option in ChatGPT. Open ChatGPT and it should prompt you to view automatically. Look in the sidebar to pull it back up at anytime.

**Your Year with ChatGPT** is a new annual review feature, similar to Spotify Wrapped, launched by [OpenAI](https://techcrunch.com/tag/openai/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-38-merry-christmas) to summarize your interactions with the chatbot over the past year.

### Key Features

- **Personalized "Awards":** Users receive titles based on their usage patterns, such as "Creative Debugger" for those who use the tool to brainstorm or solve problems.

- **Custom Content:** The experience generates a unique **poem** and an **AI-generated image** based on your most frequent topics of interest.

- **Interactive Access:** You can trigger the experience by asking ChatGPT directly for "Your Year with ChatGPT" or by clicking the prompt on the home screen.

 🧠******AI Term of The Week:****Catastrophic Forgetting **

**Note:**I mean, what a name for an AI Term (one that I unfortunately, can relate to).

 Catastrophic forgetting happens when an AI model "forgets" what it previously learned after being trained on new information like studying for a new exam and suddenly blanking on everything from the last one. This is why you can't just keep training the same model indefinitely without careful techniques to preserve old knowledge.

- Major challenge in continual learning and fine-tuning

- Why foundation models are typically trained once on massive datasets rather than updated continuously
