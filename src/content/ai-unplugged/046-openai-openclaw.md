### 🧠 AI Term of The Week: Stateless

**Definition:** A stateless system handles each request independently, with no memory of previous interactions. Each call starts fresh, as if nothing came before it.

**Explain Like I'm 5:**Imagine calling customer support, but the rep has no notes from your last call. Every single time you call, you start completely from scratch — they don't know your name, your issue, or anything you discussed before.

**Real examples:**

- **Not stateless**: Claude remembering your name, preferences, and previous questions throughout a long conversation

- **Stateless:**A basic chatbot that greets you with "How can I help?" every time, with zero memory of past sessions

- **Not stateless:**A shopping site that keeps your cart saved and knows your order history when you return

- **Stateless:** A standard LLM API call — the model receives only what you send in that single request and has no knowledge of anything prior
