### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week: Mode Evaluations (Evals)**

### 📏 What Are Model Evaluations?

 Model Evaluations are essentially **report cards for AI models**. They test how well a model performs on specific tasks, so we know its strengths, weaknesses, and reliability. Evaluations show whether your AI system is trustworthy.

### 🔍 Why Evaluations Matters in RAG Systems

 In a **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** such as a Q&A bot for your podcast transcripts, evaluations can tell you (as the builder):

- 📌 Did the answer come from the retrieved document?

- ✅ Was the answer factually correct?

- ⚠️ Did the model avoid “hallucinating” new information?
