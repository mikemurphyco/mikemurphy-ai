### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week:**Automation

**Automation** as it relates to AI Tools is setting up software to automatically perform tasks that you would normally do manually, without you having to be there to trigger each step.

**Common examples:**

- When someone fills out a contact form on your website, their information automatically gets added to your email list and they receive a welcome email

- When you get tagged in a social media post, it automatically saves to a spreadsheet for tracking

- When you receive a payment, it automatically updates your accounting software and sends a thank-you email to the customer

 The key is that automation eliminates repetitive, predictable tasks by having software do them for you based on triggers (like "when this happens") and actions (like "do that"). It's like setting up dominoes - once you push the first one, the rest fall in sequence without any additional effort from you.
