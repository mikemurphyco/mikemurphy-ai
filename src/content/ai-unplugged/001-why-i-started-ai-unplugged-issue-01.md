## **Introducing: AI Unplugged**

 This newsletter marks a clear mindset shift. I'm planting my flag and ending the BS I've been telling myself about my progress as a solopreneur and content creator. Yes, I work extremely hard and show up daily. Yes, I create more tutorials than any person should. But I've been "waiting" for doors to open instead of simply pushing through the ones staring me in the face. This newsletter launch officially marks my new chapter, and I couldn't be more excited for where I'm headed.

**AI Unplugged** is my way of documenting this journey — learning AI, building digital products, and finally transforming my content creation into a real business (one that actually generates revenue).

 It's not about AI trends or hype. It's about practical tools, real projects, and honest insights for creators who want to build something meaningful — and make a good living doing it.

 I'm not an AI expert. I'm a creator figuring it out — just like you. Let's learn together.

### 📘 AI Term of the Week:

**What is AI (Artificial Intelligence)?:**

- **Definition:** AI is a branch of computer science that aims to create machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence

- **Goal:**To mimic human cognitive functions like learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and understanding language.

- There is **Narrow AI **(designed for specific tasks…think ChatGPT, Claude, etc) and **General AI (AGI) **(similar to human intelligence. This doesn't exist yet…this is the scary phase.

- Origins in the 1950s with the term coined by John McCarthy
