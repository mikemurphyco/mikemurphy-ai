### 🗞️******AI News of The Week:****Grokipedia vs. Wikipedia**

**In Short**: Elon thought [Wikipedia ](https://www.wikipedia.org/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-31-grok-ollama-the-art-of-inference)went woke and got too biased, so he created his own unbiased version called Grokipedia. Of course, the Elon-haters say Grokipedia is biased, so maybe if you mix the two, you can find some truth.

[Grokipedia](https://grokipedia.com/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-31-grok-ollama-the-art-of-inference) is an AI-generated online encyclopedia developed by xAI that launched on October 27, 2025, as a rival to Wikipedia. Founder Elon Musk positioned it as an alternative to "purge out the propaganda" from Wikipedia, but early reception noted that many articles were copied from Wikipedia and promoted right-wing perspectives, conspiracy theories, and Musk's personal views.

**Key Facts About Grokipedia**

- Type: AI-generated online encyclopedia.

- Owner/Developer: xAI, founded by Elon Musk.

- Content Creation: Entries are created and edited by the Grok large language model (LLM).

- Launch Details: Launched on October 27, 2025, with over 800,000 articles.

### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week: Inference**

**Inference is when an AI model (LLM) makes something from your input. **After a model is trained (on tons of data), it doesn’t “think”…it infers. That means it uses what it learned to generate an output: a video, an image, a sentence, a sound. Every time you type a prompt or upload a photo and the tool gives you something back? That’s inference.

 For creators, inference is where ideas turn into results.
