### 🧠 AI Term of The Week: RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

 RAG is a way to make AI models smarter without retraining them from scratch. Instead of relying only on what the AI “knows” from training, RAG lets it search an external database or document set in real-time, then use that fresh info to generate a response.
For creators, this means:

- **Better accuracy** for niche topics

- **Dynamic updates** without retraining

- **Custom knowledge bases** for your audience or business

 If you’ve ever wished ChatGPT “knew” your notes or project files — RAG is the magic behind that.

**Note:**My next big project will be diving into RAG as I am going to build a knowledge base of all of my content (video transcripts, notes, docs, podcast transcripts, articles) and RAG will be the magic sauce for this.
