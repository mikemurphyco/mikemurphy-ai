 Hi, my name is Mike Murphy 👋.

 I've been making tutorials consistently for nearly 10 years, but lately I've been soul searching about my direction online and the future content creation business.

 Earlier this week, I asked ChatGPT to help me explain my purpose—something I’ve always felt but struggled to put into words.

 What came back genuinely blew me away.
**ChatGPT said I'm like a lighthouse because:**

- It doesn't move, but it changes everything around it.

- It *guides* without shouting. It just stays on, reliable and calm.

- It’s built in solitude, but its whole purpose is to *help others find their way.*

 This hit deep. I never mentioned to ChatGPT my affinity for lighthouses (which I've had ever since Uncle D told me I'd make a great lighthouse keeper – which I would!).

 I don't make tutorials because my calling is to teach tech tools. I make tutorials because I love helping people who are lost (in the fog).

### 🧠 AI Term of the Week:

**What does GPT stand for in ChatGPT? **You’ve heard the name a thousand times...
But do you actually know what the letters mean?

**GPT** = **Generative Pre-trained Transformer**

- **Generative** → It creates things (text, images, code...)

- **Pre-trained** → It was trained on massive data *before* you used it

- **Transformer** → A type of neural network really good at understanding language

 🧩 **Fun Fact**:
The original *GPT-1* launched in 2018. ChatGPT is based on **GPT-3.5** and **GPT-4** — the newer, much smarter versions.
