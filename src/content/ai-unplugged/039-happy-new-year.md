 🧠******AI Term of The Week:****Human In The Loop (HITL)**

 If you're building workflows in [n8n ](https://n8n.io?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=issue-39-happy-new-year)(or any automation tool), you need to understand Human-in-the-Loop.

**The concept:**Human-In-The-Loop (HITL) means AI handles the heavy lifting, but humans make the final call on critical decisions.

**In practice with n8n:**

- AI drafts your email → you review and approve before sending

- AI processes customer requests → you confirm complex refunds

- AI suggests social media posts → you give final approval

**Why it matters:**You get 80% of automation's speed with 100% of human judgment where it counts. It's the sweet spot between "fully manual" and "completely hands-off."
