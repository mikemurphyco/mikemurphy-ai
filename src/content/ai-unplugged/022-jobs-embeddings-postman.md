🧠******AI Term of The Week:****Embeddings**

 Embeddings are how AI turns words into numbers (also known as vectors) so it can understand what you mean.

 When using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) like uploading your own PDFs or notes into a chatbot, embeddings are how your content gets indexed so the AI can “remember” and “retrieve” the right stuff later.

 🔑 **Creators:** Understanding embeddings is key to building smarter AI-powered tools, bots, and assistants.
