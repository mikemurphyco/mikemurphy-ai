### 🧠 **Word of the Week: Generative AI**

**Generative AI** is a type of artificial intelligence that can *create* things—like writing, images, music, or video—based on what it’s learned from data.
Think of tools like [**Midjourney**](https://www.midjourney.com/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=ai-unplugged-issue-no-05) (which turns text into images) or [**ChatGPT**](https://chatgpt.com/?utm_source=aiunplug.io&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=ai-unplugged-issue-no-05)(which writes and answers questions)—both are powered by generative AI.
Instead of just analyzing info, it builds something new from scratch.

### 🧠 AI Tool of The Week: Custom GPT

**Custom GPTs** are personalized versions of ChatGPT that you can tailor to do specific tasks or follow certain instructions.
Think of them like mini-assistants with a custom personality, purpose, or workflow.
For example, you could build a GPT that speaks in your brand voice, helps you write social posts, answers customer questions, or guides someone through a checklist—without needing to code.
