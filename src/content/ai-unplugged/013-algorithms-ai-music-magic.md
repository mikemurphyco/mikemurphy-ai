### 🧠 AI Term of The Week: Algorithm

 At its simplest, an algorithm is a set of instructions or rules used to solve a problem or perform a task.

 In AI, algorithms power everything from recommending your next video to generating a brand new image or song. Creators do not need to code them, but understanding them helps you think like an AI-powered creator.
