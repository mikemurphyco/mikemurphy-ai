### 🧠 **AI Term of The Week:**Machine Learning (ML)

**Machine Learning** is the foundation of modern AI.

 At its core, ML is about feeding data into algorithms so they can **learn patterns** and **make predictions or decisions** — without being explicitly programmed for each task.

 Everything from voice cloning to video generation to smart playlists relies on machine learning.

**The takeaway?**
When someone says "AI did that," it's almost always **ML under the hood.**
