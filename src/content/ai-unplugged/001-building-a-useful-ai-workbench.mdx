## AI News

The useful shift is workflow, not novelty. The tools are changing fast, but the durable work is learning how to connect them without turning your day into a pile of half-finished experiments.

## AI Tool

n8n MCP is the kind of tool that makes an AI assistant feel less like a chat box and more like a practical collaborator. Codex can inspect workflows, understand available nodes, and help build automations without passing JSON back and forth by hand.

## AI Term

Tool-calling means the model asks an external tool for structured help. It might search docs, read a file, call an API, or inspect a workflow, then use that result to decide the next step.
