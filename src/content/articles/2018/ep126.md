### Twitter Fundamentals

You can follow anyone on Twitter. You must be followed by a user in order to send someone a direct message on Twitter. Each post of 280 characters or less is called a Tweet. The @ symbol is for mentions to direct a tweet at someone specifically Hashtags = \# and are used for topics You can like someone’s Tweet by pressing the heart or you can re-tweet a Tweet and share it with your audience which is a greate way to get exposure. The Twitter logo bird is named Larry.
