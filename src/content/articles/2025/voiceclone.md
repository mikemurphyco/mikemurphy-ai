**How To Clone Voices Using Eleven Labs**

1.  Open a web browser
2.  Visit <https://mikemurphy.co/elevenlabs>
3.  Create New Account or sign in
4.  Upgrade account to Starter Plan for Instant Voice Clone (used in this tutorial)
5.  Upgrade account to Creator Plan for Professional Voice Clone
6.  Click on ’Go To App’
7.  Hover cursor on left side of website to expand left sidebar
8.  Click on + Voices
9.  Click on ‘Instant Voice Mode’
10. Record at least 10 seconds of audio or drag/drop audio files.
11. Add several minutes for better results
12. Click ‘Next’
13. Enter ‘Name’
14. Label (English)
15. Description
16. Tick Box for Copyright Acknowledgement
17. Click ‘Save Voice’
18. Click ’Generate Speech (TTS)
19. Type or Paste Text for Script
20. Choose Cloned Voice
21. Click ‘Generate Speech’
22. Preview
23. Customize Voice settings to taste
