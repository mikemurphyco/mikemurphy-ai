**How To Add loopOut Cycle Expression:**

1.  Create New Comp
2.  1920 x 1080, 24 fps, 10 secs
3.  Add Footage (graphics, text, etc)
4.  Animate (Add Keyframes) on Position Property
5.  Press Option or Alt Key and click on Stopwatch next to Position
6.  Enter loopOut(“cycle”) to endlessly loop entire animation

**How To Control Which Keyframes Loop At End of Animation:**

1.  Click on loopOut Expression
2.  Add a number after “cycle” for how many keyframes to loop from the last keyframe
3.  loopOut(“cycle”, 1) will loop the last 2 keyframes after the last keyframe
4.  loopOut(“cycle”, 2) will loop the last 3 keyframes after the last keyframe
5.  loopOut(“cycle”,3) will loop the last 4 keyframes after the last keyframe
