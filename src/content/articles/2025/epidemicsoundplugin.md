**How To Install Epidemic Sound Plugin in Adobe After Effects CC 2025:**

1.  Open Adobe Creative Cloud Desktop App
2.  Click on ‘Stock & Marketplace’ icon in the left sidebar
3.  Click on ‘Plugins’ in the top category
4.  Tick ‘After Effects’ to sort plugins to only show plugins for After Effects
5.  Quit After Effects (if open)
6.  Click ‘Install’
7.  Open After Effects
8.  Open Composition
9.  Go to ‘Window Menu’
10. Click on ‘Extensions’
11. Click on ‘Epidemic Sound’
12. Click Hamburger Menu (3 horizontal lines)
13. Login To Epidemic Sound account
14. Search for Music or Sound Effects
15. Click Thumbnails to Preview/Pause
16. Click Ellipses…to ‘Import To Timeline Playhead’
17. Have fun!
