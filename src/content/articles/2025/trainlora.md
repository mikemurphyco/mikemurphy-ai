**How To Train A LoRa**

1.  Go to [https://replicate.com](https://replicate.com)
2.  Sign in with GitHub account
3.  Go to ‘Dashboard’
4.  Click on ‘Trainings’
5.  Click on Fast-Flux Trainer
6.  Destination: Create New Model (for example…aihandyman-lora-v1)
7.  Upload .zip folder of images
8.  Create Trigger_Word (i.e. mkupy)
9.  Click ‘Create Training’
10. Click on Trained Model
11. Enter Trigger Word + Prompt
12. Cmd + Enter
13. Have fun!
