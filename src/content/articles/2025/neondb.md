🪜 \*\*How To Create New Neon Account & Project

1.  Visit [https://neon.com](https://neon.com/) 
2.  Click 'Sign Up'
3.  Sign up using Google Account
4.  Check Email & Activate Account
5.  Enter Project Name
6.  Cloud Service Provider: AWS
7.  Region: N. Virginia
8.  Click Create
9.  Go To Settings & Change Organization Name

🪜 **How To Create a PostgreSQL Database on Neon**

1.  Click 'Branches' in the sidebar
2.  Click on 'Production (default)' branch
3.  Click on 'Roles & Databases'
4.  Click 'Add Database'
5.  Enter Database Name (no hyphens!)
6.  Select Owner (neondb_owner)
7.  Click 'Create'
8.  Click Plus(+)
9.  Click 'Create Table'
10. Enter Table Name (mmu_podcast)
11. Click 'Add Columns' and repeat
12. Click 'Review & Create'
13. Click 'Add Record'
14. Add data to cells
15. Click 'Save 1 Change'
16. Repeat
