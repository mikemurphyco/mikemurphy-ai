## Ep162: \# The 10-Year Anniversary & AI Roadmap

**Episode Summary:**  
Join me for a very special 10-year anniversary episode as I reflect on a decade of content creation and the story behind 155 consecutive podcast episodes and my exciting pivot into AI technology. From Adobe tutorials to RAG systems, discover how I evolved from Tech Handyman to RAG Handyman and what's next for the Mike Murphy Content Factory.

### 🎙️ **The Podcast Origin Story**

- Launching Mike Murphy Unplugged in September 2015
- The challenge of writing 3,500-word scripts weekly
- 155 consecutive episodes before taking a much-needed break
- Career opportunities that came from podcasting consistency

### 🎥 **Content Creation Journey**

- Building 1,700+ YouTube videos over 10 years
- The legendary 365-video challenge in 2017
- Evolution from Tech Handyman to specialized expertise
- Balancing audio, video, and written content across platforms

### 🤖 **The AI Transition**

- Making the scary pivot from Adobe tutorials to AI content
- Why Mike went "full nerd" on artificial intelligence
- Learning Python, terminal commands, and automation workflows
- Building an AI playground on a virtual private server

### 🧠 **RAG Systems Deep Dive**

- **RAG Explained:** Retrieval-Augmented Generation for content creators
- Building AI knowledge bases from your existing content
- Real-world applications for creators and small businesses
- The Mike Murphy Content Factory concept

### 🔧 **Becoming the RAG Handyman**

- Evolution of identity: Tech → Podcast → AI → RAG Handyman
- Mission: Making complex AI systems accessible and practical
- Target audience: Content creators and small businesses
