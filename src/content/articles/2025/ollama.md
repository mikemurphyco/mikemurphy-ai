Ollama AI Chat Assistant runs locally on computer so your data remains private.  
Upload docs or PDFs and get help writing copy, blog posts, scripts, brainstorming, etc  
No internet connection required (work on a plane or wherever you have your computer)

🪜**THE STEPS:**

**How To Install Ollama On Your Mac:**

1.  Go to [https://ollama.com](https://ollama.com/)
2.  Click on 'Download'
3.  Select Mac or OS if not automatically selected
4.  Click the 'Download’ button
5.  On a Mac, double-click on the .dmg installer
6.  Drag Ollama in the Applications Folder
7.  Double-click to open Ollama
8.  Agree to permissions
9.  Enter Mac Password
10. Ollama interface opens
11. Enter a text prompt
12. Wait for AI Model to download and install
13. Drag & Drop text or PDF documents
14. Chat with your Ollama AI Assistant
15. Change the model to try out different LLM's
16. Have fun
17. Remember, it's private and free!
