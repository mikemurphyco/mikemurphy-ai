**How To Use Text To Sound Effects on ElevenLabs**

1.  Open a web browser
2.  Visit <https://mikemurphy.co/elevenlabs>
3.  Create New Account or sign in
4.  Click on ’Go To App’
5.  Hover cursor on left side of website to expand left sidebar
6.  Click on ‘Sound Effects’
7.  Enter Text Prompt
8.  Click to Change Duration
9.  Change Prompt Influence (trial and error)
10. Click Generate
11. Play to Preview
12. Click Download to Use

**How To Access Eleven Labs Sound Effects Library:**

1.  Click on ‘Explore’ in top navigation menu
2.  Browse By Category
3.  Search for Sound Effects
4.  Select ‘Use’ to add to Prompt Bar
5.  Click on ‘Download’ to download
