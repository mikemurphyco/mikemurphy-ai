**How To Animate Images in MidJourney Library:**

1.  Go to [https://midjourney.com](https://midjourney.com)
2.  Click on ‘Create’
3.  Hover cursor over any image
4.  Click on ‘Animate’
5.  4 versions of the animation are generated
6.  Default duration is 5 seconds
7.  Hover cursor to preview
8.  Hover cursor and select ‘Extend Auto or Manual’ to make animation longer

**How To Upload & Animate Images in MidJourney Library:**

1.  Go to [https://midjourney.com](https://midjourney.com)
2.  Click on ‘Create’
3.  Click ‘Add Images’ in Prompt Bar
4.  Select Image
5.  Enter text prompt describing motion
6.  Add - - raw at end of prompt for more control
7.  Click ‘Submit’ arrow
8.  4 versions of the animation are generated
9.  Default duration is 5 seconds
10. Hover cursor to preview
11. Hover cursor and select ‘Extend Auto or Manual’ to make animation longer
