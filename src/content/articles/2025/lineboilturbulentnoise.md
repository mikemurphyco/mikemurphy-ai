**How To Add & Animate Turbulent Displace:**

1.  Create New Comp
2.  1920 x 1080, 24 fps, 10 secs
3.  Add Footage (graphics, text, etc)
4.  Create A New Solid Layer (Cmd/Ctrl + Y)
5.  Go to Effects & Presets Panel
6.  Search for Turbulent Noise
7.  Drag it onto Solid Layer
8.  Go to Effect Controls Panel
9.  Twirl open Evolution Options
10. Option + Click on Random Seed
11. Enter time\*4
12. Tap Space

**How To Add Displacement Map Effect:**

1.  Create a New Adjustment Layer
2.  Go to Effects & Presets Panel
3.  Search for Displacement Map
4.  Go to Effect Controls Panel
5.  Click Drop-down menu and select ‘Solid Layer’
6.  Click Drop-down to the right and select ‘Effects & Masks

**Finishing Touches:**

1.  Change time\* Expression on Turbulent Noise for different animation styles
2.  Adjust Opacity of Turbulent Noise to fine tune animation
3.  Change Displacement Map for distortion
4.  Drag Comp into another Composition to add background, etc
