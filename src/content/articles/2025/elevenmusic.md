**How to Generate Songs From Text using ElevenLabs Music**

1.  Open a web browser and visit [https://try.elevenlabs.io/kuhb3vjoi7gw](https://try.elevenlabs.io/kuhb3vjoi7gw)
2.  Sign in
3.  Select 'Creative Platform' in the drop-down menu on the left sidebar
4.  Select to change number of variants
5.  Set duration to Auto or change to preset
6.  Enter text prompt describing the song you want
7.  Click 'Generate'
8.  Go to Timeline and edit clips duration or change order
9.  Click in any section to modify using text prompts
10. Click Download to download song
11. Click 'Share' to create and audiogram that is customizable
