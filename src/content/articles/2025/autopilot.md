**How To Use AutoPilot on Neural Frames:**

1.  Go to [https://mikemurphy.co/neuralframes](https://mikemurphy.co/neuralframes) and create a free account
2.  Click on ‘Music icon’ in the left sidebar (AutoPilot)
3.  Click to Upload or Drag & Drop Song (from [Suno.com](http://Suno.com))
4.  Review Placeholder Artwork (click to change)
5.  Click to Edit Title
6.  Drag edges of audio waveform to trim
7.  Click inside of Lyrics to edit
8.  Click the gear icon
9.  Change Aspect Ratio (vertical or horizontal videos)
10. Click ‘Create Clip’
11. Edit Video Concept + Style
12. Click ‘Regenerate Scenes’
13. Click ‘Create Video’
14. Scroll below video to edit Scene + Clicks
15. Click to edit text
16. Hover over thumbnail to Regenerate Click
17. Go to Library to review and edit projects
18. Click thumbnail to change cover art
19. Click ‘Open Editor’ for fine tuning
20. Click video to preview
21. Click on 3 dots to ‘Download’
22. 22\. Have fun!
