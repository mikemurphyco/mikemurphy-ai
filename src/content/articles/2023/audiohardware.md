## Adobe Audition CC 2023: How To Setup Audio Hardware Preferences

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to set up the default inputs and outputs in the Audio Preferences Settings of Adobe Audition CC 2023.

Meter Input Signal: Opt/Alt + i

**Audio Hardware Preferences:  
Default Input:**  
Source of sound going into Audition (Microphone, Audio Interface, Mixer, RodeCaster Pro, USB Microphone)

**Default Input:**  
Source of sound going out of Audition (Headphones, External Monitors/Speakers, Computer Speakers)

**How To Set Audio Preferences in Adobe Audition CC 2023:**

1.  Open Adobe Audition
2.  Go To Audition in the top menu (or click Cmd/Ctrl + Comma to open)
3.  Click on ‘Settings’
4.  Select ‘Audio Hardware’ in the sidebar
5.  Click on the drop-down menu next to Default Input
6.  Select the device controlling the gain of the microphone
7.  Click on the drop-down next to Default Output
8.  Click on the device that controls the headphones or speakers
9.  Click ‘OK’

**  
How To Enable Audio Levels (Meter Input Signal)**

1.  Go to View Menu
2.  Go to ‘Metering’
3.  Click on ‘Meter Input Signal’
4.  Or use keyboard shortcut Opt/Alt + i to view audio level meters
