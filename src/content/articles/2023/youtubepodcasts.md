## YouTube: How To Submit Podcast RSS Feed

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to submit a podcast RSS Feed to YouTube to be listed in the YouTube Podcasts Directory.

**Note:  
**Email Address Must Be in RSS Feed to verify podcast ownership.

**How To Create A Podcast:  
**[YouTube Help Articles](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/12751636?hl=en&ref_topic=12751231&sjid=12655022977002490952-NA)

**YouTube will not:**

- Distribute your podcast to other platforms. Your podcast will only be available on YouTube and YouTube Music.
- Notify subscribers when back catalog episodes are added to your channel through an RSS feed.
- Automatically update show details made to your RSS feed. [Learn how to edit podcast details](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/13973017#Editpodcastdetails).
- Automatically update audio files that are re-uploaded to your RSS feed. [Learn how to re-upload episodes](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/13973017#Reuploadanepisode).
- Allow invalid characters in podcast and episode titles or descriptions, such as “\>”,“\<", or any HTML

**How To Submit Podcast RSS Feed to YouTube:**

1.  Sign into YouTube Channel
2.  Go to YouTube Studio (<https://studio.youtube.com>)
3.  Click on ‘Content’ in left sidebar
4.  Click on ‘Podcasts’
5.  Click on ‘New Podcast’
6.  Click on ‘Create a New Podcast’ to create new playlist
7.  Click on ’Set Existing Playlist As Podcast’ to search playlists and set as podcast
8.  Click on   ’Submit RSS Feed’ to submit RSS Feed URL
9.  Add Details of Podcast
10. Add Cover Art (Square 1:1)
11. Click on ‘Connect to RSS Feed’
12. Read EULA
13. Tick box ‘I agree’
14. Click ‘Accept’
15. Enter RSS Feed URL
16. Click ‘Send Code’ for verification
17. Check Email and copy code
18. Paste Verification Code
19. Choose Upload Option (All Episodes in Feed)
20. Set Visibility to ‘Public’
21. Click ‘Save’
22. RSS Feed is now connect
23. Congrats!
