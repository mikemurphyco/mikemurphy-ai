## Screenflow 10: How To Insert Speech Clips

**Description:**

This tutorial will show you how to Insert Speech Clips in Screenflow 10 which allows you to enter text and select a voice to read back the text. This tutorial also includes samples of all 145 Voices available in Speech Clips.

**2 Parts of Video:  
**0 - 2:00 mins: How To Use Speech Clips (Tutorial)  
2:00 – 7:00 mins: Samples of Voices (145 total)

**Chapters:  
**00:00  Intro: About this tutorial  
00:40  How To Insert Speech Clips  
00:57  Enter Text in Dialogue Box  
01:22  Edit Speech Clips  
01:44  Speech Clip = Audio Clips  
01:58  End of Tutorial (Part 1)  
02:21  Part 2: Speech Clip Sample Library (145 Voices)

**Software Used:  
**Screenflow 10 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/screenflow))

**How To Export Audio Only  in Screenflow 10 (Lossless-Uncompressed)**

1.  Go to File Menu
2.  Click on Export
3.  Click on Manual
4.  Click on the Preset Drop-down menu
5.  Select ‘Lossless-Audio only’ for an uncompressed AIFF file
6.  Click OK
7.  Name File
8.  Choose Destination
9.  Click ‘Export’

**  
How To Export Audio Only  in Screenflow 10 (AAC - Compressed)**

1.  Go to File Menu
2.  Click on Export
3.  Click on Manual
4.  Click on the Preset Drop-down menu
5.  Select ‘AAC-Audio only’ for an uncompressed AIFF file
6.  Click OK
7.  Name File
8.  Choose Destination
9.  Click ‘Export’
