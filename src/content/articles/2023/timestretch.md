**How To Use Time Stretch in Adobe After Effects CC 2023:**

1.  Open a Composition
2.  Add Layers of Animation to speed up or slow down
3.  Select Layer
4.  Right-Click or go to Layer Menu
5.  Select Time…Time Stretch
6.  Drag % Slider to the Right to Slow Down (higher %)
7.  Drag % Slider to the Left to Speed Up (lower %)
8.  Click Ok
9.  Tap Spacebar to Preview

**How To Add Stretch To Layers Panel:**

1.  Right-click on Header area in Layers Panel
2.  Select ‘Stretch’
3.  Click on % to open Comp Settings or,
4.  Drag to left to speed up
5.  Drag to right to slow down
