**How To Delete Frame Animation Timeline in Adobe Photoshop CC 2024:**

1.  Open Photoshop
2.  Open Timeline Panel (Window…Timeline)
3.  Add Frames to Timeline
4.  Go to Hamburger Menu (3 horizontal lines)
5.  Select ‘Convert to Video Timeline’
6.  Go back to Hamburger Menu (3 horizontal lines)
7.  Select ‘Delete Timeline’
8.  TimeLine has now been deleted and reset
