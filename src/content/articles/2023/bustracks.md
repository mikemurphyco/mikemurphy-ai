## Adobe Audition CC 2023: How To Add Bus Tracks

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to add Bus Tracks in the Multitrack so you can add Effects & Processing on the Bus Track and control the effects of multiple tracks with the Output set to the Bus Track.

- One Bus Track to Add Effects & Processing to Multiple Tracks.

**Bus Tracks:**  
A Mono, Stereo, or 5.1 Track that can be added in the Multitrack of Adobe Audition. Any effects and processing added to the Bus Track can affect any other track that has the ‘Output’ routed to the Bus Track.

**How To Add Bus Tracks Adobe Audition CC 2023::**

1.  Create a new Multitrack Session (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
2.  Add Audio to Tracks
3.  Go to ‘Header Controls’ and select the Arrows for ‘Inputs/Outputs’
4.  Look for the left facing arrow (Output) and click on the drop-down menu
5.  Select Bus…Add Bus…Mono (spoken word) or Stereo (Music, SFX)
6.  Rename Bus Track by Clicking in the Header
7.  Reposition by clicking the left side (color label) and dragging to new position
8.  Add Effects or Presets on the Effects Rack (Windows…Effects Rack)
9.  Press Spacebar to Playback Multitrack

**Note:** Bus Tracks can also be added from the Multitrack Menu (up top) and then Tracks…Add Mono Bus
