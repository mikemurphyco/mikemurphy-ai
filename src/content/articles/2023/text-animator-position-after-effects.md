## Adobe After Effects CC 2023: How To Add Position Text Animator

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to add the Text Animator Color Fill to create a position property animation in After Effects CC 2023.

**AE Version Required:  
**23.4 or Later**  
**

**Font Used in Tutorial:  
**<https://fonts.google.com/?query=londrina>

**00:00  Intro: About This Tutorial

01:30  Side Tip: Hiding Visibility of Animator  
01:51  Range Selector

**Software Used:  
**Adobe After Effects CC 2023 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe))

**How To Add Text Layer**

1.  1.  Open New Composition
    2.  Click on T to add Text
    3.  Use Properties Panel to change Font & Size
    4.  Select Text with Selection Tool (V)
    5.  Use Align Panel to center text in Composition

**How To Add Position Text Animator:**

1.  1.  Select Text Layer
    2.  Go to Properties Panel
    3.  Click on +Animator in the Animation section
    4.  Click on Color Fill..RGB

**  
How To Animate Text Animator:**

1.  1.  Go to Layers Panel
    2.  Twirl Text Layer
    3.  Twirl Open Animator 1
    4.  Change Position Y Value so text goes up and off of Comp Window
    5.  Drag CTI (Play-head) to the beginning of the Timeline
    6.  Click on Stopwatch next to Offset at 0%
    7.  Drag CTI (Play-head) forward 1 second
    8.  Change Offset to 100%
    9.  Tap Spacebar to Preview

**  
How To Add Finishing Touches**

1.  1.  Go to Layers Panel
    2.  Twirl Text Layer
    3.  Twirl Open ‘Advance’
    4.  Go to ‘Randomize Order’
    5.  Change Shape to Smooth
    6.  Tap Spacebar to Preview
