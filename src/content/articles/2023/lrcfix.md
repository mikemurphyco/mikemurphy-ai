**How To Check For Error Message in Adobe Lightroom Classic on MacOS:**

1.  Open Adobe Lightroom Classic on MacOS Mojave or later
2.  Wait until app loads and if you get an error message about ‘access to some Standard folders’
3.  Then check to see if there are exclamation marks in thumbnails indication ‘Missing Photos

**  
How To Fix Adobe Lightroom Classic Standard Folders Error on MacOS:**

1.  Click on the Apple Icon in the top-left corner of Mac
2.  Click on ’System Settings’
3.  Click on ‘Privacy & Security’
4.  Click on ‘Files & Folders’
5.  Twirl open Adobe Lightroom Classic
6.  Confirm all Folders & Volumes are toggled on (blue)
7.  Back out to previous menu
8.  Click on ‘Full Disk Access’
9.  Skim all applications and confirm Lightroom Classic is missing
10. Go to the very bottom and click on Plus (+)
11. Enter MacOS  Password
12. Locate Adobe Lightroom Classic in Applications Folder
13. Select and click Open
14. Click on ‘Quit & Reopen’ in the pop-up window
15. Error Message Should Be Gone

**  
How To Fix Exclamation Mark (Missing Photos):**

1.  Right-click on Photo Library Folder in Lightroom Library
2.  Click on ‘Find Missing Folder’
3.  Navigate to main Lightroom Folder on your hard drive
4.  Select & click ‘Choose’
5.  All Exclamation Marks should be gone
6.  Quit Lightroom Classic
7.  Re-open Lightroom Class
8.  Error Message Popup and Exclamation Marks should be gone

**  
How To Add Permissions for Adobe Lightroom CC on MacOS:**

1.  Click on the Apple Icon in the top-left corner of Mac
2.  Click on ’System Settings’
3.  Click on ‘Privacy & Security’
4.  Click on ‘Full Disk Access’
5.  Skim all applications and confirm Lightroom is missing
6.  Go to the very bottom and click on Plus (+)
7.  Enter MacOS  Password
8.  Locate Adobe Lightroom CC in Applications Folder
9.  Select and click Open
10. Click on ‘Quit & Reopen’ in the pop-up window
