**How To Update To Adobe Photoshop CC 2024:**

1.  Open Adobe Creative Cloud App on computer
2.  Click on ‘Apps’ in the top-left corner
3.  Click on 'Check For Updates' in the top-right corner
4.  Click 'Update All'
5.  Tick the 2 Boxes in the Updates Dialogue Box
6.  Click ‘Continue’
7.  Click ‘Open’ next to Photoshop to open in Adobe CC App or,
8.  Open Finder/Explorer and open Photoshop CC 2024

**  
Adobe CC 2024 Featured + Categories:**

1.  In the Adobe App
2.  Go to left Sidebar
3.  Review new ‘Featured Items’ in the left Sidebar (Firefly + Express)
4.  Click each link to open in a web browser
5.  Click on ‘Agree’ for Adobe Firefly
