## Adobe After Effects CC 2023: How To Use Wigglerama Effect

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you use The Wigglerama Effect to add random movements to an entire Composition or Pre-Comp in Adobe After Effects CC 2023.

**Wigglerama Settings Used in Tutorial:  
**Wiggle Speed: 0.5  
Wiggle Nervousness: 1.0  
Wiggle Position: 10  
Wiggle Rotation: 0.5  
Wiggle Scale: 0.5  
Wiggle Width: 5

**Chapters:  
**00:00  Intro: About This Tutorial  
00:13  Comp Settings  
00:24  Pre-Comp + Layer Overview  
00:57  Add New Adjustment Layer  
01:12  Add Wigglerama Effect  
01:32  Collapse Transformations  
02:12  Effect Controls (Customize Wigglerama)  
02:59  Transform Effect

**Software Used:  
**Adobe After Effects CC 2023 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe))

1.  **How To Use Wigglerama in Adobe After Effects CC 2023:**

    1.  1.  Create a New Composition
        2.  Add Layers & Pre-Comps
        3.  Animate layers as desired
        4.  Create a New Adjustment Layer
        5.  Go to Effects & Presets Panel (Windows …Effects & Presets)
        6.  Search for ‘Wigglerama’
        7.  Click & Drag on Adjustment Layer
        8.  Adjust settigs to taste
        9.  Wiggle Speed: 0.5
        10. Wiggle Nervousness: 1.0
        11. Wiggle Position: 10
        12. Wiggle Rotation: 0.5
        13. Wiggle Scale: 0.5
        14. Wiggle Width: 5
        15. Done
