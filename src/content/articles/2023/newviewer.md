## Adobe After Effects CC 2023: How To Add New Comp Viewers in After Effects

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to add New Composition Viewers in Adobe After Effects CC 2023 to help speed up your workflow by enabling you to view multiple comps/pre-comps in different viewers.

**Keyboard Shortcut  
***Add New Viewer:***  
**Shift + Option + Cmd + N (Mac)  
Shift + Alt + Ctrl + N (Windows)

1.  **How To Add New Comp Viewers in Adobe After Effects:**

    1.  Click on Composition Window
    2.  Right-Click and select New…Viewer or
    3.  Go to the View Menu & Select ‘New Viewer (Shift + Option + Cmd + N)
    4.  Rearrange Panels by clicking on top area and dragging

    **How To Lock Comp Viewers:**

    1.  Click on the Padlock next to ‘Composition’ to lock or unlock
    2.  Unlocked Viewers will show any selected Comp/Pre-Comp in the Layers Panel
    3.  Locked Viewers will show whatever is listed next to ‘Composition’ at the top of each viewer

    **How To Close Comp Viewer:**

    1.  Click on the ‘X’ in the top-right of any Viewer Window
