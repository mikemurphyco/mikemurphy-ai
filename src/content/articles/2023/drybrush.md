## Adobe Photoshop CC 2022: How To Convert Images or Videos to Paintings

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to convert images or videos to paintings using the Dry Brush in the Filter Gallery of Adobe Photoshop CC 2023

**Chapters:

02:04  Example 2: Convert To Smart Object

**Software Used:  
**Adobe Photoshop CC 2023 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe%20(affiliate%20link)))

**How To Convert To Paintings in Photoshop:**

1.  Open a photo or video
2.  Select layer in the Layers Panel
3.  Right-click and select ‘Convert To Smart Object’
4.  Go to the top menu
5.  Click on ‘Filter’
6.  Click on ‘Filter Gallery’
7.  Twirl open Artistic category
8.  Click on ‘Dry Brush’
9.  Drag sliders to get desired painterly look
10. Brush Size: 6
11. Brush Detail: 7
12. Texture: 1
13. Click ‘OK’

**  
How To ‘Bump’ up the Color:**

1.  Click on the half/moon icon below layers
2.  Click on ‘Hue/Saturation’
3.  Drag Saturation slider to the right to increase saturation

**  
How To Edit or Make Adjustments:**

1.  Double click on ‘Filter Gallery’ to open Filters
2.  Make adjustments to Dry Brush as necessary
3.  Click ‘OK’
4.  Select the Hue/Saturation Layer
5.  Make adjustments to saturation/vibrance
