##  

### **Introduction:**

- Do you have dreams of making a living teaching online and making courses?
- Have you ever wondered what it takes to make an online course for a large online learning platform such as [Domestika](https://www.domestika.org/en)?

Well, good news! I just finished creating a [course on Domestika](https://www.domestika.org/en/courses/4919-basics-intro-to-adobe-audition/mikemurphy) and I am going to share my experience from start-to-finish and give you a behind-the-scenes look at the entire process.

### ** Confession Time:**

I love making [tutorials](https://mikemurphy.com/youtube), [podcasts](https://mikemurphy.co/mmu), [writing how-to articles](https://mikemurphy.co/medium) and I’m a creative tech nerd who is very proficient in video and audio production, but for some reason, I have always struggled to make online courses.

Online courses have always been my achilles heel or kryptonite if you like, even though they were the main reason I started my [online business](https://mikemurphy.co/) in 2015. I have countless folders on my hard drive with partially-created classes and loads of course outlines in my Apple Notes ready to go. It has always been difficult to block out the required time (or so I told myself) to put together an entire course (even though I have over 1100 tutorials on my [YouTube channel](https://youtube.com/mikemurphyco)).

 

### ** Moving Forward:**

In the summer of 2022, I received an email from Domestika asking if I would be interested in creating a course. Heck yeah I would!

After several months of hard work, I broke through a big mental hurdle and created a course with the support of the amazing people at Domestika, and now I want to take you behind the scenes to show you how I did it in hopes that it may inspire you to create or finish your own course!

**What is Domestika?**

[Domestika](https://domestika.org/) is a multi-lingual online learning platform that offers courses and workshops on a variety of creative topics, such as design, illustration, photography, animation, and more. Domestika has been around for over 20 years, and is one of the largest platform for creative education in the world.

![](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/1*o9krJdFhV08HsdLo8-PfqQ.jpeg)

[Domestika](https://domestika.org/)

Domestika originated in Spain and now has over 8 million engaged students registered and are growing quickly as they expand internationally. All courses are entirely produced in-house in our state-of-the-art studios (US, Europe, and Latin America) with skilled producers, at no cost to the teachers and distributed and marketed to over 50 countries subtitled in 8 different languages.

### **Domestika: How It Started**

In the Summer of 2022, I received a email from Domestika that started off by saying the Domestika Curation Team has been following my content for some time (flattery works) and wanted to know if I would be interested in teaching a course about [Adobe Audition](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe) since I had so many tutorials on my YouTube Channel and my experience helping people launch podcasts.

I am cautious and skeptical by nature, but there was something about this email and opportunity that felt good right from the start and I knew if everything checked out then I was on-board for the ride.

**Spoiler alert:** Everything checked out and I signed a contract within 2 weeks of that initial email. My gut instinct was right. The Domestika experience from start-to-finish was top-notch and easily one of the best decisions of my professional career.

## **Creating the Class**

Domestika instructors have 100% creative freedom to build the class in their own style and voice. The Domestika team is available to help and support and provide guidance and feedback, but instructors have complete freedom when it comes to course creation.

![](https://miro.medium.com/v2/resize:fit:1400/1*ZInYX5rkIU4OPiA2-lTPbA.jpeg)

[Learn Adobe Audition From Scratch](https://youtu.be/0tGAIX7q2Cs)

**  
My Class:  
**[Introduction To Adobe Audition](https://www.domestika.org/en/courses/4919-basics-intro-to-adobe-audition/mikemurphy)

- This class is for anyone who wants to start using [Adobe Audition](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe) for podcasting, music, voiceovers, etc whether you are brand new to audio production or a seasoned professional wanting to switch DAWs.
- Basics courses are the best-selling classes on Domestika intended for beginners and broken down into ‘Mini-Courses’ with practice lessons at the end of each section.

**Here is the basic structure of the class:**

1.  Getting Started
2.  Adding Media & Recording
3.  Editing in Waveform & Multitrack
4.  Effects & Processing
5.  Exporting & Saving

### **The Final Stage (Waiting For Launch)**

- After filming was over in New York City, it is a bit of a waiting game as the it takes several months for the Domestika editors to edit and get the class ready for launch
- During this time I spruced up my Domestika Portfolio and Profile Page and finalized the course text for the website and prepared resources and PDFs to enhance the student learning experience.
- I also had several meetings with the Teacher Management Team whose role is to help instructors have a successful launch and prepare the marketing and promotion plan leading up to launch day.

 

Inconclusion, creating a course on Domestika was a challenging and rewarding experience for me.

With the support of the amazing team at Domestika, I was able to break through the my internal mental struggle of making an online course and create a course that I am proud of.

The process of creating a course for Domestika was rigorous but well worth it, and I hope that my behind-the-scenes look at the process has inspired you to create or finish your own course.

I highly recommend Domestika as a platform to showcase your expertise and share your knowledge with millions of students around the world. Thank you for joining me on this journey, and I wish you all the best in your own course creation endeavors!
