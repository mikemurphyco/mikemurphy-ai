## Adobe After Effects CC 2023: How To Use CC RepeTile For Backgrounds

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to use the CC RepeTile Effect to create animated backgrounds in Adobe After Effects CC 2023.

**Keyboard Shortcuts:  
**Move Tool: V  
Scale: S  
Rotation: R  
Position: P

**Chapters:**  
00:00  Intro: About This Tutorial  
00:28  Add Paper Texture to Composition  
00:50  Drag CTI To Beginning of Timeline  
01:03  Add Position Property Keyframe  
01:21  Preview the Animation  
01:39  Add the CC RepeTile Effect  
02:10  Adjust CC RepeTile Effect  
03:04 Sidenote: CC RepeTile Directions  
03:26  Fix the Seam! (Unfold)

**Software Used:  
**Adobe After Effects CC 2023 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe))

**How To Animate Texture Layer in Adobe After Effects CC 2023:**

1.  1.  Create New Composition
    2.  Add a layer such as a paper texture to be used as background
    3.  Select texture layer
    4.  Drag CTI to the beginning of the Timeline
    5.  Tap ‘P’ for Position Property
    6.  Click the stopwatch next to Position to add a keyframe
    7.  Drag CTI to the end of the Timeline
    8.  Move texture file in the direction of the desired animation so it is just off the canvas
    9.  Tap SpaceBar to preview the animation

**How To Add CC RepeTile Effect in Adobe After Effects CC 2023:**

1.  1.  Create New Composition
    2.  Add a layer such as a paper texture to be used as background
    3.  Select texture layer
    4.  Go to Effects & Presets (Window…Effects & Presets)
    5.  Search for ‘CC RepeTile’
    6.  Drag effect on texture layer

**How To Adjust CC RepeTile in Adobe After Effects CC 2023:**

1.  1.  Drag the CTI to the center of the timeline so the texture is half on the canvas and half off
    2.  Go to Effect Controls panel where CC Repetile was added
    3.  Change the properties in the direction to extend the texture (Extend Right, Left, Up, Down)
    4.  Keep moving CTI and adjusting the CC RepeTile Extend values until the end of the animation
    5.  Press Spacebar to preview

**How To ‘Fix’ the seam in the repeated texture:**

1.  1.  Go to Effect Controls Panel
    2.  Change the Tiling from Repeat to Unfold
