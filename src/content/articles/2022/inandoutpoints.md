## After Premiere Pro CC 2022: How To Set & Clear In & Out Points

 

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to Set & Clear In & Out Points in the Source Window of Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2022. In & Out Points are used to make a selection of footage that is to be placed on the Sequence Timeline.

**Software Used:  
**Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2022 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe%20(affiliate%20link)))

**Keyboard Shortcut:**

- Set In Point: i (the letter i)
- Set Out Point: o (the letter O)
- Clear In Point: Option or Alt + i
- Clear Out Point: Option or Alt + o
- Clear In & Out Points: Option or Alt + X

**How To Set In Points in Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2022:**

1.  Double click on the clip to open in Source Window
2.  Drag the playhead marker to where you want to add the In Point
3.  Tap the keyboard shortcut ‘i’  
     

**How To Set Out Points in Adobe Premiere Pro CC 2022:**

1.  Drag the playhead marker to where you want to add the Out Point
2.  Tap the keyboard shortcut ‘o’ (the letter O)

**How To Clear In & Out Points in Premiere Pro CC 2022**

1.  Go to the Source Window 
2.  Set In Point (i) and Set Out Point (o)
3.  Press the Option Key (Mac) or Alt Key (Windows) plus the letter X

**How To Clear In Point Only **

1.  Press the Option Key (Mac) or Alt Key (Windows) plus the letter i

     

**How To Clear Out Point Only **

1.  Press the Option Key (Mac) or Alt Key (Windows) plus the letter o
