## Adobe Audition CC 2022: How To Use Multitrack Mixer

**Description:**  
This tutorial will show you how to use the Mixer in Adobe Audition CC 2022 which is another way to view and manage a Multitrack session and to make it easier to adjust the volume/gain of tracks and add automation. The Multitrack Mixer does not show audio clips but does show all of the tracks as channels similar to a hardware mixer.

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**

**Multitrack View:** 0 (zero)\|  
**Waveform View**: 9  
**Show Mixer View:** Option or Alt + 2

**Software Used:  
**Adobe Audition CC 2022 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe))

**How To Use The Multitrack Mixer:**

1.  Open a Multitrack Session
2.  Click on ‘Mixer’ at the top of the Multitrack or click on Option/Alt + 2  
    (Click on Window in the top menu and select Mixer if not visible)
3.  Track Names are at the bottom in Mixer View (click to change)
4.  Master Track is at the far right of Mixer
5.  Press Spacebar to preview the audio
6.  Drag sliders up and down to adjust volume/gain
7.  Click ’S’ to solo a track
8.  Doubleclick on the slider ‘knob’ to reset to zero
9.  Add Effects to Effect Rack 
10. Click on Read or Write to add automation
11. Click on ‘Editor’ to return to Multitrack
