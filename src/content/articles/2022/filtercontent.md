## Screenflow 10: How To Filter by Content Type and Duration

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to filter or sort clips and footage in the Media Bin by the Type of Content and/or the Duration (minimum and maximum durations).

**Content Types:**  
Movie  
Audio  
Image  
Screen Recording  
Unknown

**Software Used:  
**Screenflow 10 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/screenflow))

**How To Filter Clips & Footage by Content Type in Screenflow 10:**

1.  Open Media Bin (last icon in the Inspector Panel
2.  Click on the left-facing arrow at the very bottom of the Media Bin
3.  Select ‘Filter’ in the panel that slides open
4.  Click on ‘Content Type’ and check the boxes you want to filter and Screenflow will only show the content type with a checkmark next to it.

**How To Filter Clips & Footage by Duration in Screenflow 10:**

1.  Open Media Bin (last icon in the Inspector Panel
2.  Click on the left-facing arrow at the very bottom of the Media Bin
3.  Select ‘Filter’ in the panel that slides open
4.  Check the box next to ‘Duration’
5.  Enter min and max duration and Screenflow will only show clips that fit the criteria.
