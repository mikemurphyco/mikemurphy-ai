## After Effects CC 2022: How To Reveal Comps & Layers in Project Bin

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to save custom Composition Presets in Adobe After Effects CC 2022 so you can quickly create a new Composition with the settings you previously saved by selecting a Preset from a drop-down menu.

**Keyboard Shortcuts:  
Create a new Composition:  
**Cmd + N (Mac)  
Ctrl + N (Win)

**Software Used:  
**Adobe After Effects CC 2022 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe%20(affiliate%20link)))

**How To Save Composition Presets in After Effects**

1.  Create a New Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
2.  Start with a Preset or manually enter settings
3.  Enter Width x Height (for example, 1080 x 1920 px)
4.  Pixel Aspect Ratio: Square
5.  Frame Rate: 24 or 30 fps
6.  Resolution: Full
7.  Start Timecode: 0:00;00;00
8.  Duration: 0:00;10.00 (10 seconds for example)
9.  Background Color: Any color you want
10. Click on the folded paper icon
11. Enter a Name for Preset (TikTok for example)
12. Click ‘Ok’ 
13. Saved custom preset is now in the drop-down for quick access

**How To Delete a Saved Composition Presets in After Effects**

1.  Create a New Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
2.  Click on the Preset drop-down menu
3.  Select the Preset to be deleted
4.  Click on the Trash icon next to the drop-down menu
