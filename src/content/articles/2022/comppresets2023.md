**How To Access Composition Presets in After Effects CC 2023**

1.  Open After Effects
2.  Create A New Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
3.  Click on the drop-down menu next to ‘Preset’
4.  The HD Presets are at the top (1920 x 1080)
5.  Then the new UHD Presets (4K, 8K)
6.  Below are the new Social Media Channel Presets (Portrait, Landscape, Square)
7.  Select any preset
8.  Modify the settings
9.  Click OK

**How To Save Custom Composition Presets in After Effects CC 2023**

1.  Open After Effects
2.  Create A New Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
3.  Click on the drop-down menu next to ‘Preset’
4.  Select any preset 
5.  Modify the settings
6.  Click on the folded paper icon next to the drop-down menu
7.  Enter a name for a custom preset
8.  Click OK to Save Custom Preset
9.  Select custom preset from the drop-down menu to apply to any new Composition
