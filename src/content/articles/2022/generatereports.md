## After Effects CC 2022: How To Collect Files To One Footage Folder

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to generate project text file reports in Adobe After Effects CC 2022. Project text file reports include all of the details of an Adobe After Effects project including source file names and drive location, Comp & Pre-comp names, Effects and Fonts used. Reports could be very useful to share with team members or collaborators or for students to learn how a project is made.

**Note:  
**Project Reports will be generated in the same drive location or folder as the After Effects project file.

**How To Collect Files in Adobe After Effects CC 2022:**

1.  Go to the top menu in After Effects
2.  Click on ‘File’
3.  Click on ‘Dependencies’
4.  Click on ‘Collect Files’
5.  Click ‘Save’ 
6.  Select ‘All’ in the drop-down menu for ‘Collect Source Files’
7.  Check the box ‘Generate Report Only’  
8.  Check ‘Reveal collected project in finder when finished’
9.  Click the ‘Comments’ button
10. Type a comment if you to save it in the text report
11. Click on Save’
12. Enter the title of the Report
13. Click ‘Save’
14. Review Footage Folder 
15. Double-click to open the Report in a text editor
16. Zip/Compress Folder (optional)
17. Ready to Share
