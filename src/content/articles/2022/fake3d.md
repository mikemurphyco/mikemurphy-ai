**How To Create Faux 3D Text Extrusion Effect in AE CC 2022:**

1.  Create New Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
2.  Add Text to Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + T)
3.  Right-click on the text layer and click on Create…’Convert Text to Shapes’
4.  Delete the original text layer to keep things neat (optional)
5.  Twirl open ‘Outlines’ layer
6.  Twirl open ‘Contents’
7.  Click on ‘Add’
8.  Select ‘Group’
9.  Select all letters and drag them into ‘Group’
10. Click ‘Add’ and select ’Stroke’
11. Click ‘Add’ and select ’Repeater’
12. Change Stroke Color
13. Change Stroke Width to 10px
14. Change Line Cap to Round Cap
15. Change Line Join to Round Join
16. Twirl open ‘Repeater’
17. Change Copies from 3 to 40
18. Twirl open ‘Transform Repeater’
19. Change X & Y Position Property to 1
20. Change X to -1 to Reverse Shadow Direction
21. To change the extrusion amount, change the number of copies

**How To Animate Faux 3D Extrusion Effect**

1.  Drag playhead to the beginning of the timeline
2.  Change the Repeater number of copies to 10
3.  Click on the stopwatch to add a keyframe
4.  Move the CTI (playhead) forward in time 1-2 seconds
5.  Change the Repeater number of copies to 40
6.  Copy the last keyframe
7.  Move CTI forward 1/2 second and paste
8.  Move CTI forward 1/2 second and paste
9.  Move CTI forward 1 second and change copies to 10
10. Press Spacebar to Preview
