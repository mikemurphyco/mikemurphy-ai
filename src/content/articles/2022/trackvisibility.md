## Adobe Audition CC 2022: Track Visibility (Show & Hide Tracks)

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to use Track Visibility in Adobe Audition CC 2022 to show and hide tracks in the Multitrack Editor and how to create and load custom view presets using the Tracks Panel.

**Chapters:  
**00:07 Intro: What you will learn  
00:27 1. Track Visibility Menu: Editor  
00:37 How to hide empty tracks (No Audio Clips)  
00:54 2. Multitrack Menu: Track Visibility...Show All Tracks  
01:11 3. Tracks Panel (Windows Menu)  
01:45 How to save Presets (Custom Views)  
01:59 Show only tracks under the CTI  
02:30 How to Load Presets

**Software Used:  
**Adobe Audition CC 2022 ([Click To Try or Buy](https://mikemurphy.co/adobe))

**How To Hide Tracks using Track Visibility Menu (Method 1):**

1.  Open a Multitrack Session
2.  Click on the hamburger menu (3 horizontal lines) next to Editor
3.  Click on ‘Track Visibility’
4.  Click on ‘Show Only Tracks With Clips’ to hide empty tracks

**How To Show All Tracks using Track Visibility Menu (Method 2):**

1.  Open a Multitrack Session
2.  Click on the Multitrack Menu up top
3.  Click on ‘Track Visibility’
4.  Click on ‘Show All Tracks’ to show all tracks including tracks with no audio clips

**How To Show & Hide Tracks using the Tracks Panel (Method 3)**

1.  Open a Multitrack Session
2.  Go to Window…Tracks to open the Tracks Panel
3.  Click on the ‘Eyeballs’ to show and hide individual tracks with or without audio clips
4.  Click on the hamburger menu (3 horizontal lines) 
5.  Click on ‘Track Visibility’
6.  Select any option to show and hide tracks

**How To Save Custom View Presets using the Tracks Panel**

1.  Open a Multitrack Session
2.  Go to Window…Tracks to open the Tracks Panel
3.  Click on the ‘Eyeballs’ to show and hide individual tracks 
4.  Click on the hamburger menu (3 horizontal lines) 
5.  Click on ‘Track Visibility’
6.  Click on ‘Save …Preset 1’ to save a custom view

**How To Load Custom View Presets using the Tracks Panel**

1.  Open a Multitrack Session
2.  Go to Window…Tracks to open the Tracks Panel
3.  Click on the ‘Eyeballs’ to show and hide individual tracks 
4.  Click on the hamburger menu (3 horizontal lines) 
5.  Click on ‘Track Visibility’
6.  Click on ‘Load… Preset 1’ to load a custom view
