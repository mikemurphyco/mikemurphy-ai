## After Effects CC 2023: Navigate Keyframes on Selected Layers & Properties

**Solids Folder Basics**

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you the keyboard shortcuts to navigate (jump to) keyframes on all layers or only on selected layers or properties in Adobe After Effects CC 2023.

**Keyboard Shortcuts:  
**U:  Tap ‘u’ key to show all keyframes  
K:  Move to next keyframe (forward)  
J:  Move to the previous keyframe (reverse direction)  
Shift + K:  Move to next keyframe on a selected layer or property (forward)  
Shift + J:  Move to the previous keyframe on a selected layer or property (reverse direction)

**To Jump from Keyframe to Keyframe (All Layers):**

1.  Press ‘K’ to move to the next keyframe (forward)
2.  Press ‘J’ to move to the previous keyframe (reverse direction)

**To Jump from Keyframe to Keyframe (Selected Layers):**

1.  Select a layer or multiple layers
2.  Press ‘Shift + K’ to move to next keyframe on selected layer(s) (forward)
3.  Press ‘Shift + J’ to move to previous keyframe on selected layer(s) (reverse direction)

**To Jump from Keyframe to Keyframe (Selected Layer Property):**

1.  Select a layer property or multiple layer properties (ex. Position, Scale, Rotation, etc)
2.  Press ‘Shift + K’ to move to next keyframe on selected properties (forward)
3.  Press ‘Shift + J’ to move to previous keyframe on selected properties (reverse direction)
