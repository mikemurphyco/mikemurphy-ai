## Notion 2022: How To Change Dark or Light Mode (Desktop)

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to change the Appearance Settings in Notion to Dark Mode, Light Mode, or System Settings on a desktop. Note: You can also change the appearance of mobile devices.

**Notion Version:** 2.0.21 (June 2022)

**Light Mode:** Notion appearance will be ‘Light’  
**Dark Mode:** Notion appearance will be ‘Dark’  
**System Setting:** Notion appearance will take on the system settings of the computer (light or dark mode)

**Keyboard Shortcuts:  
Notion Preferences:  
**Cmd + Comma (Mac)

**Software Used:  
**Notion ([Link to Try](https://notion.so))

**How To Change Notion Appearance Settings:**

1.  Click on Settings & Members or Open Preferences (Cmd + Comma)
2.  Click on ‘My notifications & settings’
3.  Scroll down to the ‘My Settings’ section
4.  Look for ‘Appearance’
5.  Go to the far right and click downward facing arrow (v)
6.  Select ‘Dark Mode’ to change Notion’s appearance to Dark Mode
7.  Select ‘Light Mode’ to change Notion’s appearance to Light Mode
8.  Select ‘Use system setting’ to use computer System Preferences
