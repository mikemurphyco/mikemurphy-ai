## How To Replace Apple Watch Bands

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to replace the watchband on an Apple Watch.

**My Apple Watch:**  
Apple Watch SE:  
<https://www.apple.com/apple-watch-se/>

**My Apple Watchband:**  
41mm Blue Jay/Abyss Blue Sport Loop  
<https://apple.co/38pfefb>

**Software Used:  
**[Ecamm Live](https://mikemurphy.co/ecammlive) (to schedule and Livestream)

**How To Replace Apple Watchband**

1.  Line Up New Watchband with the existing watchband to visually see how to attach
2.  Turn Apple Watch Face over so you are looking at the back
3.  Press the Top Button on the back of the Apple Watch
4.  Slide the old watchband off
5.  Slide the new watchband into the groove until you hear and feel a little click 
6.  Pull on the watchband to confirm it is securely attached
7.  Click on the bottom button
8.  Slide the old watchband off
9.  Slide the new watchband into the groove until you hear and feel a little click 
10. Pull on the watchband to confirm it is securely attached
11. Easy as that!
