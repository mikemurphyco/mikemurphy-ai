# Ep 14: The Learn Show

In today’s episode, we’re gonna get on the school bus and learn where I do all my studying online. The places I go to most often to learn about: Tech, podcasting, business, design, photography, video and more. **Ask Mike Anything:** **Today’s Ask Mike Anything** comes from a question I received on Blab this past Monday night from a high school teacher who asked, *Are there any good resources online to teach my students about podcasting.* **Resources Mentioned:** [Free Podcast Course by John Lee Dumas ](http://https//itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/free-podcast-course-podcast/id947671706?mt=2)[Mike Murphy Unplugged](http://mikemurphy.co/itunes) **The Cons of Learning Too Much:** Being a jack of all trades, not knowing what your good at, not practicing what your learning and fear of starting something.

### **WHERE I LEARN**

**[Lynda.com](http://lynda.com/) (\$35/mo)** The best. \$35/month.
