# EP13: The Resource Show

1.  Branding: (This is the big category)
2.  Photos (Stock Photos)
3.  Video Footage & Animations (Intros, Lower Thirds..)
4.  Audio (For podcasts and video projects)
5.  Outsourcing

## **1. Branding & Graphics**

Logos Fonts Colors Graphics.

### **Logos:**

1. [TailorBrands.com](http://tailorbrands.com) (about \$50) 2. [Squarespace Logos](http://www.squarespace.com/logo/) (\$10) 3. [99 Designs.com](http://99designs.com) 4. [48hourlogo.com](http://48hourlogo.com)

**Bonus Tip:** Logo Inspiration: [Dribbble.com](http://dribbble.com) or [LogoPond.com](http://logopond.com) and just browse for ideas.

### **Fonts:**

***Where do I go for fonts?:***

[Adobe Typekit:](http://https://typekit.co/foundries/adobe) [MyFonts.com](http://myfonts.com) [FontSquirrel.com](http://fontsquirrel.com) (My favorite free source): [League of Moveable Type](http://https://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/) (for my hipster fix) [Google Fonts:](http://www.google.com/fonts) [DaFont.com](http://dafont.com)

Pairing Fonts: [TypeGenius.com](http://typegenius.com)

**Bonus Resource:** [100 Free Fonts 2015](http://https://goo.gl/JkwwXZ)

### **Colors:**

**My go-to resources for colors:** 1. [Adobe Color CC](http://color.adobe.com) (Used to be Kuler) 2. [Coolors.co](http://coolors.co) 3. My super secret tool: [0to255.com](http://0to255.com). This is great for getting variations of a color you like really fast.

### **Graphics & Templates:**

[Adobe CC Marketplace](http://https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=2&ved=0ahUKEwjLhJXX-7vJAhXHez4KHc45AbMQFgggMAE&url=http%253A%252F%252Fwww.adobe.com%252Fcreativecloud%252Fassets.html&usg=AFQjCNGI4KtMYUxUc-zvKSRh-TQT5qgZRA&sig2=_ZR7uLBX484oIgOsEk8oLw) (Tons of free stuff inside all CC applications) [Adobe Exchange](http://www.adobe.com/mena_en/exchange/) [CreativeMarket.com](http://mikemurphy.co/creativemarket) [GraphicRiver.net](http://graphicriver.net) [Canva.com](http://canva.com)

**Bonus tip:** [Canva.com](http://canva.com) is a great place to scope out trendy new fonts

[![Creative market](/assets/media/2015/08/300x250-E-300x250.jpg)](http://creativemarket.com?u=videounplugged)

**\*\*Warning:** With any stock resource, Respect copyright and do not just google a photo and use it because it works. It is your responsibility to only use photos you have the right to use and simply linking the source or pleading ignorance is not going to cut it\*\*
