**How To Use Find & Replace Text in Adobe After Effects CC 2024:**

1.  Open a Composition
2.  Add Text Layers (Cmd/Ctrl + T)
3.  Select Text Layers to Find & Replace
4.  Go to the File Menu
5.  Click on ‘Scripts’
6.  Click on ‘Find & Replace Text.jsx’
7.  Enter text you want to search for  in ‘Find Text’ field
8.  Click ‘Find All’ to Narrow Search To Only Source Text listed in ‘Find Text’
9.  Enter text in ‘Replacement Text’ field to replace the text in ‘Find Text’
10. Click ‘Replace All’
11. Click ‘?’ to learn more about the Find & Replace Text script
