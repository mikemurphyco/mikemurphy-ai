## Adobe After Effects CC 2024: How To Reverse Paste Keyframes

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how Reverse Paste Keyframes to reverse play an animation in Adobe After Effects CC 2024.

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**   
Cmd/Ctrl + C: Copy Keyframes  
Shift + Cmd/Ctrl + V: Reverse Paste Keyframes  
UU: Reveal All Keyframes

**Version Used In Tutorial: **  
This feature was released in May 2024 (Version 24.4.0 or later)

**How Copy & Paste Reversed Keyframes in Adobe After Effects CC 2024:**

1.  Create New Composition (1920 x 1080, 10 seconds)
2.  Add Graphic such as logo
3.  Animate on Position, Rotation and/or other properties.
4.  Select Layer & Tap UU To Reveal All Keyframes
5.  Drag a selection over keyframes
6.  Copy To Clipboard (Cmd/Ctrl + C)
7.  Move CTI forward in Time
8.  Paste Reversed Keyframes (Shift + Cmd/Ctrl + V) or go to Edit Menu…Paste Reversed Keyframes
9.  Tap Spacebar to Preview
10. Animation should play in forward direction and then reverse direction on the Pasted Reversed Keyframes
