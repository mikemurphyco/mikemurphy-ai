**How To Use Audio Spectrum Effect:**

1.  Create New Composition
2.  Add Media with Audio
3.  Create a New Solid Layer (Cmd/Ctrl + Y)
4.  Go to the Effects & Presets Panel
5.  Search for ‘Audio’
6.  Drag ‘Audio Spectrum’ on top of the Solid Layer
7.  Go to Effect Controls
8.  Go to Audio Layer Property
9.  Select Layer that contains audio
10. Customize the Audio Spectrum Effect in ‘Effect Controls’

**  
How To Animate Circle Shape Progress Bar:**

1.  Twirl open Shape Layer (Audio Player)
2.  Twirl open Circle Shape Group
3.  Twirl open Ellipse Path
4.  Drag CTI to the beginning of the Timeline
5.  Click on the Stopwatch next to Position
6.  Drag CTI to the end of the Timeline
7.  Change Position Value until the Circle Shape reaches the end
8.  Drag CTI to the beginning
9.  Tap Spacebar
