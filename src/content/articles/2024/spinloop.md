**How To Add Spin-Loop Animation Preset in Adobe After Effects CC 2024:**  
1. Create Composition  
2. Add Logo (or any graphic)  
3. Go to Effects & Presets Panel (Window..Effects & Presets)  
4. Twirl open ‘Adobe After Effects 2024 folder  
5. Twirl open ‘Presets’ folder  
6. Twirl open ‘Adobe Express’ folder  
7. Twirl open ‘Looping’ folder  
8. Select ‘Spin Loop’  
9. Drag on to layer  
10. Tap Spacebar to preview animation  
11. Go to ‘Effect Controls’ panel to customize preset (change Personality, Direction, Intensity)

**How To Remove Animation Presets from Layers:**  
1. Select Layer  
2. Go to ‘Effects’ menu at the top  
3. Click on ‘Remove All’
