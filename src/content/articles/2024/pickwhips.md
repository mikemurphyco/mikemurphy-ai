## Adobe After Effects CC 2024: How To Use Pick Whips

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to use three different types of Pick Whips in Adobe After Effects CC 2024 to create Parent & Child Relationships between Layers, Properties & Expressions.

**Pick Whip:**   
Look for the spiral icon in the Layers Panel that looks like the end of a ‘lasso or rope’ that you can use to ‘link’ elements to one another to create ‘Parent & Child’ relationships. The ‘child’ layers inherit the Parent attributes.

**Types of Pick Whips:**  
**Parent:**  
Create parent-child relationships between layers

**Property:**  
Link properties between different layers or effects

**Expression:**  
Create expressions that reference other properties

**How Link Layers Using Parent Pick Whip:**

1.  Create New Composition
2.  Add at least 2 layers
3.  Go to the Layers Panel
4.  Right-click the Header
5.  Click on ‘Columns’ and then ‘Parent & Link’
6.  Click on the Pick Whip Icon of 1 Layer & Drag to another layer or
7.  Click on the drop-down menu under ‘Parent & Link’ and select Parent Layer.
8.  Child Layer will ‘follow’ the Parent Layer.

**How To Use Property Pick Whip:**

1.  Create New Composition
2.  Add at least 2 layers
3.  Twirl open Transform Properties on both layers
4.  Go to Rotation, Scale, or Opacity
5.  Click on the Pick Whip Icon next to Rotation Property (for example)
6.  Drag Pick Whip next to Rotation to RotationProperty of the other layer
7.  Change Rotation of the ‘Parent’ layer and the ‘Child’ will change Rotation

**How To Use Expression Pick Whip (example from tutorial):**

1.  Create New Composition
2.  Add Logo Layer
3.  Tap P for Position
4.  Right-click on Position and ‘Separate Dimensions’
5.  Add Expression Slider Control & Duplicate for a total of 4
6.  Rename Slider Controls for Position X&Y and Wiggle (frequency, amplitude)
7.  Option/Alt + Click on X Position Stopwatch
8.  Select Expression Pick Whip and drag on the X Slider Control
9.  Option/Alt + Click on Y Position Stopwatch
10. Select Expression Pick Whip and drag on the Y Slider Control
11. Tap R for Rotation
12. Option/Alt + Click on Rotation Stopwatch
13. Type wiggle(2,20)
14. Highlight frequency (2) value
15. Click on Expression Pick Whip and drag to Frequency Slider Control
16. Highlight Amplitude (20) value
17. Click on Expression Pick Whip and drag to Amplitude Slider Control
