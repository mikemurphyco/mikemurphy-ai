**How To Use Align Layers Adobe After Effects CC 2024:**

1.  Open Align Panel (Window…Align)
2.  Select Layer(s)
3.  Go to Align Panel
4.  Change ‘Align Layers To’ either Selection or Composition
5.  Click on Align Tools (Align Left, Horizontally Center, Right, Top, Vertically Center, Bottom)

**How To Use Distribute Layers Evenly Adobe After Effects CC 2024:**

1.  Open Align Panel (Window…Align)
2.  Select 3 Layers or More
3.  Go to Align Panel
4.  Change ‘Align Layers To’ either Selection or Composition
5.  Click on Distribute Evenly Tools (Distribute…Top, Vertically, Bottom, Left, Horizontally, Right)
