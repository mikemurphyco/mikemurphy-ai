## Adobe After Effects CC 2024: How To Get Inner & Outer Strokes on Shape Layers

**Description:  
**This tutorial will show you how to create an authentic crumpled paper texture animation in Adobe After Effects CC 2024 for a stop-motion or hand-drawn look.

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**  
V: Selection Tool (move position of textures)  
W: Rotation Tool (rotate textures)  
S: Scale (change scale of layers)  
Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + D: Split Layers  
Cmd/Ctrl + Right Arrow (move CTI one frame at at time)

**How To Crumple Paper & Scan:**

1.  Take any piece of paper you want to animate as background
2.  Crumple paper & then open so it can be placed on scanner or photographed
3.  Take photo and AirDrop or send to computer or,
4.  Scan using 600 dpi and save as JPEG

**How To Import Crumpled Paper Texture:**

1.  Open After Effects
2.  Create New Composition (Cmd/Ctrl + N)
3.  Composition Settings: 1920x1080, 24fps, 5 seconds
4.  Double-click on empty space in Project Panel to open Finder/Explorer
5.  Select Paper texture file scanned to computer
6.  Click ‘Open’
7.  Drag Texture on Composition

**How To Animate Crumpled Paper Texture:**

1.  Select Layer
2.  Tap Cmd/Ctrl + Right Arrow 4 times to move CTI 4 frames
3.  Press Shift + Cmd/Ctrl + D to split layers
4.  Repeat until there are 12 layers (or however many you want)
5.  Press N to set Work Area on the last frame
6.  Go to bottom layer
7.  Tap V for Selection Tool and move layer position
8.  Tap W for Rotation Tool and change the rotation of layer
9.  Repeat randomly changing position and rotation for each layer
10. Tap Spacebar to Preview Animation

**Finishing Touches:**

1.  Add Adjustment Layer
2.  Add Posterize Time Effect (fps to 4)
3.  Add CC Vignette Effect to darken edges
4.  Add Grain (uncheck color noise, set to 10%)
5.  Add Text & Graphics (Hand-drawn Effect)
