**How To Add Motion Blur**

1.  Open After Effects Composition
2.  Create an animation to add Motion Blur (Position Property works well)
3.  Click on Motion Blur icon above the Layers Panel (stacked circles) to enable Motion Blur for the entire Composition
4.  Right-click on Layer Panel Header row and select ’Switches’
5.  Check the box under Motion Blur icon
6.  Motion Blur has been added to layer
7.  Tap Spacebar to preview the animation

**How To Change Motion Blur Settings:**

1.  Go to Composition Menu at the top
2.  Click on ‘Composition Settings’ (Cmd/Ctrl + K)
3.  Click on ‘Advanced’
4.  Increase Shutter Angle to increase Motion Blur
5.  Decrease Shutter Angle to decrease Motion Blur
