## **Priorities of 2016:**

**Goal:** Improve podcast quality and take audio production skills to next level **Action:** Ask for feedback and help in G+ and study and practice.

**Goal:** Make more video and written tutorials and improve video skills **Action:** One video tutorial per week commitment and continue to study video editing.

**Goal:** Increase networking and build relationships in podcast community **Action:** Serving hours on social media and direct contact

**Goal:** Drive more website traffic and grow email list **Action:** Increase number of lead magnets & CTAs

**Goal:** Re-purposing my content **Action:** Make blog posts and tutorials out of 2015 episodes. Use Blab content

**Goal:** Monetize **Action:** More product videos for affiliate links & courses

### **Social Media Plan:**
