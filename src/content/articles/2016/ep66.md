**Episode 66** is an introduction to Adobe Creative Cloud Mobile Apps What are the [Adobe Creative Cloud Mobile Apps](https://www.adobe.com/products/catalog/mobile._sl_id-contentfilter_sl_catalog_sl_mobiledevices.html) and how can they help your creativity and workflow for your podcast, brand and online business? **Todays sponsor:** [PodcasterToolkit.co](http://podcastertoolkit.co) (I’m getting there, promise). ### What is Adobe Creative Cloud?

[Adobe Creative Cloud or Adobe CC](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud) is a bundle of creative software applications like Photoshop and Illustrator and Premiere Pro and Audition. **Pricing** Pay one price of [\$49.99/month](https://creative.adobe.com/plans?plan=team&promoid=KSRSV) for all programs Pay [\$19.99](https://creative.adobe.com/plans?plan=team&promoid=KSRSV) for a la carte programs Pay \$9.99 for [The Photographer Bundle](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud/photography.html?promoid=KLXML) that includes Photoshop, Lightroom & Bridge. Education discounts available

### CC Libraries

[Adobe Creative Cloud Libraries](https://goo.gl/uh4lra) are nothing more than a Drop Box for anything you want access to across all of the programs for the most part. In the past if you were designing a marketing piece you had to go find your logo and colors and all of your branding assets. With [CC Libraries](https://goo.gl/uh4lra) you can store them all in one folder that shows up in Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, etc and you just drag them into your project. You can put almost anything in there such as photos, vectors, brushes, patterns, color swatches. Really a time saver and so is creating on your iDevices, so let’s go
