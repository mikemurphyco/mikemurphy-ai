# Ep39: The Google Show

What is [Google Apps](https://apps.google.com/)? What features do I use regularly? What are some unique ways podcasters and online brands can use [Google Apps](apps.google.com/‎) for their podcast or online business?

### Ask Mike Anything:

**Question:** Do I need expensive headphones to record my podcast or are Apple earbuds good enough? **Answer:** I do recommend you upgrading from the Apple earbuds if possible to headphones that will give you a flatter/cleaner sound so you can really hear any issues in your recording. \**Tip:*\*Use your Apple buds for quality control as many of your listeners use them so you need to know how your show sounds on many different devices. **My headphones** [Sony MDR7506](https://www.mikemurphy.co/sonymdr7506) **Popular or Recommended Headphones** *[AudioTecnica ath-m50x](https://www.mikemurphy.co/athm50x)*[M-Audio Q40 headphones](https://www.mikemurphy.co/q40) \*[Beyerdynamic dt 770](https://www.mikemurphy.co/dt770)

### What is Google Apps For Work?

Googles description: An all-in-one suite to communicate, store and create. [Google Apps](https://apps.google.com/) is the admin side of your business like email, calendar, word processing, spreadsheets and so much more. All the real fun and exciting stuff I know, but all the stuff that keeps your business on track.

### How much does it cost:

\$5/user per month. There is a \$10/user per month with increased storage and some additional features.

### What is included with [Google Apps](https://apps.google.com/) :

**The communication apps:** \* [Gmail](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/gmail/) \* [Calendar](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/calendar/) \* [Google Hangouts](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/hangouts/) \* [Google +](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/googleplus/) **Storage app:** [Google Drive](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/drive/) (similar to Google Drive) **Collaboration tools:** \* [Google Docs](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/docs/) \* [Sheets](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/sheets/) \* [Forms](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/forms/) \* [Slides](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/slides/) \* [Sites](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/sites/) **\[Collaboration Tips\]**(https://apps.google.com/learning-center/tips/collaborate-better/#/list) **Tip:** **[Email Aliases:](https://support.google.com/domains/answer/6304345?hl=en)** Create unlimited prefixes/alials such as mike@mikemurphy.co; info@mikemurphy.co (hello@, feedback@…) **[Domain Alias:](https://support.google.com/a/answer/53295?hl=en)** Use other domains you own and create email address for them under same account: For example: mike@askmikeanything.com….

### What [Google Apps](apps.google.com/‎) features do I use for my podcast and online business?

[Gmail](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/gmail/) [Calendar](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/calendar/) [Google Drive](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/drive/) [Google Docs](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/docs/) (Docs, Sheets, Forms, Slides) [Google +](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/googleplus/)

### Some ideas for [Google Apps](apps.google.com/‎) for podcasters

1.**Use [Google Sheets](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/sheets/) (or Excel)** Create master episode list with notes and topics discussed Create resource lists of products and tools, links, etc 2.**[Google Forms](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/forms/)** Create surveys and feedback forms or have contests with audience (vote on a new feature… **Resource tip \#1:** [Wufoo](http://wufoo.com) **Resource tip \#2:** Check out [IFTTT.](https://ifttt.com/) 3.**[Collaboration & Sharing.](https://apps.google.com/learning-center/tips/collaborate-better/#/list)** \* Eliminate the back and forth dialog of emails. \* Great for coaching and consulting with clients. \* Send Google Forms with questions pre-interview to guests \* Collaborate on scripts or show notes with co-hosts… \* Google Docs for virtual assistants which are big in the online world. 4.**[Google Slides](https://apps.google.com/intx/en_us/products/slides/)** Make keynote presentations & screencasts
