### ep63: The Adobe Lightroom Show:

Photo organization and workflow using Adobe Lightroom & Lightroom Mobile.

### What exactly is Adobe Lightroom?

How much does it cost? What is my photo organization and workflow system and how does it help me with my online business?

### So, What is Adobe Lightroom:

Adobe Lightroom is a photo editor and an organizer OR manager of photos and videos for Mac or PC It is included in the Adobe Creative Cloud bundle. Lightroom was officially launched in 2007 mainly to help professional photographers organize and edit large quantities of high-resolution and raw photos quickly and proficiently. **Tip \#1:** Back up your Lightroom catalogs regularly. **Tip \#2:** Shoot in RAW if you can. **Quick visual for you here:** I keep all of my photos on an external hard drive which are constantly being backed up to [Backblaze.com](http://mikemurphy.co/backblaze) for \$5/month.

### How much does Lightroom cost:

Lightroom is included in the Adobe CC bundle for \$49/month [The Photographer’s Bundle is only \$9.99](https://goo.gl/t7954X): **Includes:** Lightroom, Photoshop, Bridge and all of the Mobile Apps. ### My Workflow:

All photos live on an external drive in a folder called Photos. Inside that folder I break it down by Year & Month (2016\>November) I organize travel photos by location (Europe 2014…)

### Lightroom Mobile

Free I use the Lightroom camera when taking photos with iPhone Full functional version of Lightroom on iPhone and iPad 2-way syncing of editing and if I shoot a picture on my phone, it automatically uploads to Lightroom Only works with Collections Smart Collections do not work.
