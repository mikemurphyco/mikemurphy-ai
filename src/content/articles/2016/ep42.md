### The 10 Step Workflow:

#### 1. Verify:

Check to make sure episode is live in iTunes and in my Podcast app. **Tip:** Subscribe to your own show You can fix typos or replace audio files

#### 2. Organize & Setup Framework

Set up Wordpress blog post Create episode Artwork in inDesign Replicate newsletter template in Mailchimp

#### 3. Social Media Marketing

Use [Bufferapp](http://bufferapp.com) in Chrome to send to Facebook, Twitter and G+

#### 4. Exercise & Breakfast

Very important part of my workflow!

#### 5. Script Writing

See [Episode 40](http://mikemurphy.co/ep40) for more on my scripting process [Pomodoro Timer App](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/pomodoro-timer-focus-on-your/id872515009?mt=12) (\$2.99)

#### 6. Social Media Maintenance and Correspondence

Re-Buffer the morning posts and respond to any correspondence or 'Likes'...

#### 7. Recording & Editing

Using [Adobe Audition](http://audition.adobe.com) (New updates are awesome!

#### 8. Tagging & Uploading

[ID3 Editor](http://www.pa-software.com/id3editor/) [Libsyn](http://libsyn.com)

#### 9. Show Notes & Wordpress Scheduling

I prepare show notes partly in [Scrivener](http://mikemurphy.co/scrivener), but mostly in [Byword](https://bywordapp.com/) (a multi-markdown writing app)

#### 10. Newsletter & Topic Curation

[Mailchimp](http://mailchimp.com)
