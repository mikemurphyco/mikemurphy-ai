What steps did I take and will you take to launch [PodcasterToolkit.co](http://podcastertoolkit.co), new Wordpress site from ground zero? “To begin, begin” -William Wordsworth

You need a domain name like podcastertoolkit.co. Buy one at GoDaddy.com and pay \$8.99 for a new domain or url.

#### Step 2: Sign up for Web Hosting

#### Step 4. Organize Master Folder

Put all assets and logos and theme files in one place on your hard drive.

#### Step 5: Change Nameservers:

**Sign into BlueHost** Assign your domain name first. Copy Nameservers (ns1.bluehost.com and ns2.bluehost.com) **Sign in to GoDaddy** Delete the GoDaddy Nameservers and paste the name servers from BlueHost. They say 24 hours before it works, but it usually only takes 5–10. Back to BlueHost.
