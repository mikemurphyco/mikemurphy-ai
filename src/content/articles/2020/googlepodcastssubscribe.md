How To Subscribe & Unsubscribe to Podcasts

### Google Podcasts iOS App Tutorial:

**This tutorial will show you how to:** 1. Subscribe to Podcasts in the Google Podcasts iOS App 2. Unsubscribe from Podcasts (from both the Home Screen & Activity Page) 3. View All Subscriptions (from both the Home Screen & Activity Page) **Note:** There is a video tutorial at the bottom of the article if you want to skip the text portion.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*kBfBMnMJ0FYOB80f22ZGdg.png)

**To Download Google Podcasts iOS App:** [https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1398000105](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1398000105)

### How To Subscribe to Podcasts (Explore Page):

1.  Open [Google Podcasts App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1398000105) on iOS Device
2.  Click on Magnifying Glass icon to go to **Explore Page**
3.  Search for a podcast or browse by topic
4.  Tap the Cover Art of any show
5.  Tap the **+Subscribe** button to subscribe to the podcast

### How To Unsubscribe from Podcasts (Home Page)

1.  Open [Google Podcasts App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1398000105) on iOS Device
2.  Click on **Home** icon at the bottom of the app
3.  Subscriptions are at the very top of page
4.  Tap the Cover Art of one of your subscriptions
5.  Tap the **+Subscribed** button and pop-up will appear from the bottom
6.  Tap **Unsubscribe** to unsubscribe from the show

**Note:** You can also unsubscribe from shows on the **Activity Page** by swiping left on any subscription and tapping **Unsubscribe**

### How To View All Subscriptions (Activity Page):

1.  Open [Google Podcasts App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1398000105) on iOS Device
2.  Click on **Activity** icon at the bottom of the app (3 lines with arrow)
3.  Tap **‘Subscriptions’** in the top navigation menu to view all subscribed podcasts
4.  Swipe left on any subscription to **Unsubscribe**

**Note:** You can also view all subscriptions by clicking on the **Home Icon** and all of your subscriptions will be at the very top of the page
