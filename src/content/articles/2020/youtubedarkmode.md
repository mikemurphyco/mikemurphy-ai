Turn on Dark Theme for YouTube and Enjoy!

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*viyBt_vN2YtrT70CMGPdSg.jpeg)

Enable Dark Theme for YouTube

> This tutorial will show you how to turn on Dark Theme for YouTube in a web browser (Google Chrome).

- Dark Theme for YouTube is for your own personal viewing experience
- Dark Theme must be enabled for each browser/device you use
- Dark Theme is available on web browsers and mobile apps for iPhone, iPad, and Android.

### How To Turn On Dark Theme for YouTube (Chrome)

1.  Open Google Chrome or any web browser
2.  Sign in to YouTube
3.  Click on your Profile Image in the top-right corner
4.  Click the toggle switch next to ‘Dark Theme’ to enable dark mode/theme.
5.  Repeat steps to switch between dark and light.
6.  Enjoy Dark Mode

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*JC4Tqb58FrzjIMSz_OZohg.jpeg)

### How To Turn On Dark Theme for YouTube (iOS)

1.  Open YouTube App on iOS Device (similar for Android)
2.  Click on Profile Pic in the top-right corner
3.  Click on ‘Settings’
4.  Look for ‘Dark theme’
5.  Toggle switch on for Dark/Light
6.  Enjoy Dark Mode
