Security & Privacy Concerns? Change Your Facebook Password.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*FBK0F8DPrLORX-wmX31dew.jpeg)

How To Change Facebook Password

> Has it been awhile since you’ve changed your Facebook password?

> Do you think your Facebook page has been compromised?

> Have you noticed any unusual activity on your computer, email, or online browsing?

#### What you will learn:

- This article will show you how to change your Facebook password in a web browser and in the iOS Facebook App (iPhone or iPad).
- Video Tutorial is below the text portion if you want to skip the article.

#### Security Tools I Use Online (not required for this tutorial):

- [**1Password (\$36/year)**](https://1password.com/sign-up/)**:** My password manager of choice for many years. I highly recommend you stop using Sticky Notes and other non-secure methods to store passwords (email, Apple Notes, Google Docs, etc).
- [**Authy (Free)**](https://authy.com/)**:** Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) at your favorite sites (Facebook, Twitter, Amazon, Gmail…). This is an incredible app that makes it harder for people to get access into sights that you have 2FA enabled because an extra security step is required to verify your identity.

### How To Change Facebook Password (Web-Browser)

- If you notice any suspicious activity online or something just feels off, I recommend changing your Facebook password immediately.
- Here is how to do it using web browser such as Google Chrome

#### **Step by Step (Browser)**

1.  Go to [https://facebook.com](https://facebook.com)
2.  Log in to Facebook (click *Forgot account?* if you need to reset password)
3.  Click the down-facing arrow in the top-right
4.  Click on ‘Settings’
5.  Click on Security & Login on the left sidebar
6.  Click on ‘Change Password’ in the Login section
7.  Enter Current Password
8.  Enter New Password
9.  Re-Type New Password
10. Click ’Save Changes’
11. Check the radio button for ‘Review Other Devices’
12. Click ‘Continue’
13. Review All Devices for any suspicious activity
14. Click the 3 dots to the right to ‘Log Out’ of any device that is not current
15. Done. Facebook password has been changed

### How To Change Facebook Password (iOS App)

#### Step by Step (iOS App)

1.  Open the Facebook App
2.  Log in to the app if not already
3.  Click the hamburger menu (3 horizontal lines) in the bottom right corner
4.  Scroll down to ‘Settings & Privacy’
5.  Click on ‘Settings’
6.  Click on ‘Security & Login’ under the Security section
7.  Click on ‘Change Password’ in the Login section
8.  Enter Current Password
9.  Enter New Password
10. Re-Type New Password
11. Click ’Save Changes’
12. Done. Password has been changed from your iOS Device
