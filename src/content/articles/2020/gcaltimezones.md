### How to Add Time Zones to your Google Calendar

1.  Open Google Calendar in a web browse: ([https://calendar.google.com/calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar))
2.  Click on the ‘Settings’ icon in the top right corner
3.  Click on ‘Settings’
4.  In the General tab, click on ’World Clock’ (or just scroll to section)
5.  Tick the box for ‘Show World Clock’
6.  Click ‘Add Time Zone’
7.  Click the drop-down menu to select a time zone **Note:** There is no search (😔) so use cmd/ctrl + F to search for a time zone or city
8.  Select Time Zone
9.  Click ’Add Time Zone’ to repeat the process
10. Time Zone list will be located just below the monthly calendar in the left sidebar of Google Calendar
11. Uncheck ‘Show World Clock’ to temporarily remove the list at any time.

### How to Delete Time Zones from sidebar list:

1.  Open Google Calendar in a web browse: ([https://calendar.google.com/calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar))
2.  Click on the ‘Settings’ icon in the top right corner
3.  Click on ‘Settings’
4.  In the General tab, click on ’World Clock’ (or just scroll to section)
5.  Click on the ‘X’ next to the time zones you want to remove from the list
6.  Click ‘Add Time Zone’ to add more.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*PjLljmeX2nJomOGGqqYthg.png)

Click the ‘X’ next to any Time Zone to remove from the sidebar list

### How to Edit Time Zones from sidebar list:

1.  Open Google Calendar in a web browse: ([https://calendar.google.com/calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar))
2.  Click on the’ Settings’ icon in the top right corner
3.  Click on ‘Settings’
4.  In the General tab, click on ’World Clock’ (or just scroll to section)
5.  Click the arrow and select new Time Zone from the drop-down menu (use cmd/ctrl + F to search
6.  Select new time zone and it will change in the sidebar list

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*EqH6yWIi-B8aVMKfpaNC1g.png)

Click the downward arrow next to any Time Zone to change/edit
