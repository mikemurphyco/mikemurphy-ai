![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*KFgxfI7TQjHKwn-AFUFQDQ.jpeg)

Change the Playback Speed on YouTube Videos

> Do you want to consume more content in a shorter amount of time? Do you like listening to podcasts at a faster speed? Do you ever wish you could slow down a YouTube video to watch in more detail?

### YouTube: How To Change Playback Speed

This article will show you how to change the playback speed when watching or listening to YouTube videos in a web browser or on the mobile app (iOS for this tutorial).

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*dJg7jvQPN5ImU7f0V1vdXQ.jpeg)

Playback Speeds on [YouTube](https://youtu.be/t3Jgt59Ilps)

### How to Change YouTube Playback Speed (Browser)

1.  Open Web Browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc)
2.  Go to [YouTube.com](https://mikemurphy.co/youtube) and play video of choice
3.  Click on the Gear icon (settings) in the lower right corner
4.  Click on ‘Playback Speed’
5.  Select Slower (0.25,0.5, 0.75) or Faster (1.25, 1.5, 1.75, 2x)
6.  Select ‘Custom’ in the top right to use a slider to fine-tune the speed
7.  Repeat the process to change to another speed or return to Normal (1x)

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*VAy8WyWZRcsfl4raXm1EDw.gif)

### How to Change YouTube Playback Speed (iOS App)

**Note:** This tutorial uses iOS App, but will work similarly on Android.

1.  Open the YouTube app on iOS Device
2.  Select & Play a [Video](https://youtu.be/t3Jgt59Ilps)
3.  Tap the 3 dots in the top-right corner
4.  Tap on ‘Playback Speed’
5.  Select Slower (.25,.50, .75) or Faster (1.25, 1.5, 1.75, 2x)
6.  Repeat to change to another speed or return to Normal (1x)
