## EP156: The Change Show

In Today’s episode, I am going to read a simple, but practical checklist from a book that I recently finished to help or inspire you to create a positive change in your life. The checklist I am going to share with you today is from the book, [What to Say When You Talk To Yourself](https://amzn.to/2SqIFCO) by Shad Helmstetter which is all about rewiring your brain with positive thoughts and self-talk.
