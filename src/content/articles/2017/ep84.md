### STEP 1: Pick A Topic

**Topics:** I keep a running list of tutorial ideas on a whiteboard and: [Trello](http://trello.com) [Workflowy](http://workflowy.com) **Need Topics?** Look at your email or DM’s for questions you get Browse social media and look for discussions [Twitter Advanced Search](https://twitter.com/search-advanced?lang=en) [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/) [Quora](http://quora.com)

### STEP 2: Recording & Editing

**What is Screencasting Software?:** An application on your computer that records computer screen, iOS Devices, Webcams **Recording Tip:** Record your screen in full resolution (my retina display is 2880 x 1800 so it’s much larger than 1920x1080 and that allows me to scale down without quality loss. **Tools I Use To Record:** [Camtasia 3/9](http://mikemurphy.co/camtasia) [Screenflow](http://mikemurphy.co/screenflow) Quicktime (Free on Macs) **Tools I Use To Edit Quick Tip Videos:** [Camtasia 3/9](http://mikemurphy.co/camtasia) [![](https://show.onenetworkdirect.com/digitalriver/583094.gif?e=dbrbxrbchormwy)](http://send.onenetworkdirect.net/z/583094/CD232132/) [![](/assets/media/2017/03/Screenflow_Buy_300x250.gif)](https://store.telestream.net/affiliate.php?ACCOUNT=TELESTRE&AFFILIATE=71872&PATH=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.telestream.net)

### STEP 3: Exporting & Formatting.

**Terminology to Learn:** P=pixels HD= High Definition Frame Rate: 30 fps or 24 fps 16:9 = Wide Aspect Ratio 1:1 = Square Aspect Ratio Mp4= File Extension for Video H.264= Codec for Web Videos **Recommended Sizes for Final Video:** **YouTube:** 1920x1080p **Website:** 1920 x 1080p **Medium:** 1920 x 1080p **Facebook:** 1920 x 1080 & 1080x1080p **Twitter:** 1920 x 1080 & 1080x1080p **Instagram Posts:** 1080 x 1080 (Square) 864 x 1080 (Vertical) 1800 x 945 (Horizontal) 1920 x 1080 (Wide) **Instagram Stories:** 1080x1920 (Vertical)

### STEP 4: Sharing Online:

**Organize your Folders on Hard Drive** ***My Folder Structure:*** Assets (Graphics & Photos, etc) Thumbnails (YouTube & Covers) Audio (Music) Project Files (Camtasia & Screenflow Files) Renders (Final Videos) **AirDrop Square Video to iPhone** Write Outline in Notes App: Text Message to iPhone **Upload to [Instagram](http://instagram.com/mikeunplugged)** Square Video (1080 x 1080) Write Descriptions Use Hashtag Shortcut Share to Twitter & Facebook from Instagram **Upload to [YouTube](http://mikemurphy.co/youtube)** Wide Video (1920 x 1080) Write Descriptions Copy Embed Link **Write Post on [Medium.com](https://goo.gl/akRBFy)** Paste Link
