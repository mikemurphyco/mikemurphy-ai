# EP98: The Video Toolbox Show

Inside the Mike Murphy toolbox of gear, software and iOS apps that I use to make all of my videos. **Inspiration for show:** On a regular basis, I am asked what my favorite tools are for making videos or I am asked which piece of software I use to edit all of my videos. The reality is I use an array of gear and programs on my Mac and iPhone to make nearly every video and it really all depends on what the video needs.
