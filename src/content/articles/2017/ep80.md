### EP80: The Show Notes Show

Happy St. Patricks Day! **Episode Topic**: Behind the Scenes of Show Notes **What are Show Notes?:** This blog post. It’s the text that accompanies each podcast episode. Show Notes are where you would leave links and resources to items mention in the podcast and provide a good overview of what listeners can expect if they listen. **What you will learn** How I write my show notes How I Format my Show Notes for the Web How I Prepare my episode artwork How I Schedule & Publish Each Weekly Episode on my Wordpress Site.

### Show Notes: How To Not Hate Them

1.  Have a repeatable process
2.  Write Script or Outline with Show Notes in mind.
3.  Find balance and do not agonize over minutiae

### Mike Murphy Unplugged Show Notes Workflow:

**Step 1:** Episode Artwork & Featured Image for the Wordpress Blog Post **Tool Used:** [Adobe Indesign](http://www.adobe.com/products/indesign.html) **Time:** 5–10 minutes [![](/assets/media/2017/03/INDESIGN-150x150.png)](http://www.adobe.com/products/indesign.html)   **Step 2:**  Craft Show Notes Outline Duplicate Script Copy Show Notes to Clipboard (Cmd + C) **Tool Used:** [Scrivener](http://mikemurphy.co/scrivenver) **Time:** 10 – 20 minutes ![](/assets/media/2017/03/Scrivener-148x150.png) **Step 3:**  Multi-Markdown Formatting Paste Script Use Multi-markdown language (HTML short codes) to format Show Notes for Wordpress post **Tool:** [Byword](https://bywordapp.com/) [![](/assets/media/2017/03/BYWORD-150x150.png)](https://bywordapp.com/) **Step 4:**  Create New Blog Post in [Wordpress.org](http://Wordpress.org) using [Divi Theme by ElegantThemes.com](http://mikemurphy.co/divi) [![Divi 3.0](/assets/media/2016/10/300x250-150x150.jpg)](http://mikemurphy.co/divi) **Tip:** Add /wp-admin after your web address to log-in –Schedule the post to Publish on Fridays (for me). –Change permalink episode link to make sharing easy –Replace Episode & Featured Images   **Step 5:**  Login to my media host, [Libsyn.com](http://Libsyn.com) and copy the mp3 link and podcast layer code. [![](/assets/media/2017/03/Libsyn-150x150.png)](http://Libsyn.com) **Step 5.5:**  Paste mp3 url in [PowerPress Plugin by Blubrry](https://wordpress.org/plugins/powerpress/) [![](/assets/media/2017/03/PP-150x150.png)](https://wordpress.org/plugins/powerpress/) **Step 6:**  Prepare the newsletter in [Mailchimp](http://mailchimp.com) [![](/assets/media/2017/03/MailChimp-150x150.png)](http://mikemurphy.co/newsletter) **Step 7:** Create a promo video for each episode   **Step 8:** Use [Buffer](https://buffer.com/) to send show notes page out to Facebook, Twitter and Google Plus Rinse & Repeat.
