### Ep71: The Instagram Live Show

###### What is Instagram Live & how can you use It to promote your podcast and brand?

**Follow Me On Instagram:** [@mikeunplugged](http://instagram.com/mikeunplugged) [Episode 37 The Instagram Show](http://mikemurphy.co/ep37) Instagram had 300 million users. **Instagram 2017:** Instagram now has 600 million active users. [Episode 49 Instagram Stories](http://mikemurphy.co/ep49). Instagram stories is a 24-hour rotating story where each one of your posts gets delete at the end of 24 hours. You can post unlimited photos or 10-second videos and upload to your story and anyone can view your story but each posts will disappear when they hit the 24-hour mark. Instagram Stories is similar to Snapchat.

### How do You Use Instagram Live?

Instagram Live can be found in the same place as Instagram Stories. Open Instagram. Swipe Right and your camera screen will open. Below the shutter button, Select Live Start Live Video. Easy     **Side Hack Tip:** Live videos disappear, but if you want to save, record your iPhone with Quicktime, Camtasia & Screenflow screencasting software.

### Ways to use Instagram Live for podcasters & content creators:

Q&As Teach a lesson or tutorial/How To’s Unboxing of gear or anything Go Live at an event (bring audience along) Conduct an interview
